Collapsible Rain Barrels




Click on Thumbnails Below to Enlarge Image









  • Description
Our Collapsible Rain Barrel allows you to reuse rain water that is perfect for gardening as it is better for your plants than tap water. This rain barrel is made from flexible laminated polyester which provides superior strength, durability, and resistance to weathering. The collapsible design allows the barrel to be easily moved and stored.A 44 gallon or 58 gallon size is available.

About the Design
The Collapsible Rain Barrel is perfect for smaller garden areas. Itscompact size and versatile design allows it to be used with your gutter downspout or with a rain chain. Theincluded filter keeps out debris and insects. The barrel features an overflow tap that can be used to link two barrels together. Also included is a water tap on the front of the barrel with an on/off valve and a removable quick release hose attachment. The valve fits a standard ¾” diameter hose.Assembly, transport, and storage is made easy with the six detachable heavy duty resin legs.

About the Materials
Each Collapsible Rain Barrel is made of three layered, flexible laminated polyester with a PVC coating. This UV resistant material provides durable outdoor protection and will not puncture if used under normal conditions. The zipper top featuresreinforced stitching and all seams are welded to prevent tearing. The barrel is supported by six heavy duty resin legs.
