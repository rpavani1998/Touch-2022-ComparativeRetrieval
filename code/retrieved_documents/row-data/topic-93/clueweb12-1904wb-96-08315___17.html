Presto Cool Touch Electric Tilt' N' Drain Big Griddle Review


I am definitely a breakfast person. I love breakfast. it's my favorite meal of the day. I make breakfast pretty often for my family. Sometimes, it is the most pain in the butt meal to cook though. I'm using at least 3 pans at a time and sometimes I even use more and have to cook things once something else is done.



Then the Presto Cool Touch Electric Tilt' N' Drain Big Griddle came to the rescue and made my life so much easier. It has a huge surface, 18 1/2" x 15" to be exact. It's almost twice the size of othergriddles I've seen! I can cook our whole breakfast on here. It comes in handy on those days when I make a huge breakfast too because I can cook on this and the stove top. That way everything is cooking at the same time, perfect!

It has a Tilt'N' Drain feature which allows you to switch to a tilted surface to drain fat and grease from meats such as bacon. You can also immerse the griddle in water with the heat control removed. That makes for super easy cleanup!

Traditonal Fourth of July Celebration with Sam's Club


We do quite a few cook outs every year. Ok, I may have underestimated that just a tad. We cook out pretty often, whether it be at my house or another person's house. Sometimes we'll just all get together at the spur of the moment and have a big feast. Summertime is definitely a busy time of the year around here!

Fourth of July is one of our biggest celebrations. A huge group of family, friends and all of their family and friends get together at a local park and make a whole day of it. We fire up the grill and cook all day as people come and go. It's always such a great time and this year will be no exception. I can't wait!

Sam's Club has you covered for your Fourth of July celebration and I was able to have a preview party thanks to them. Of course, mine was a little bit scaled down compared to normal, but there was no way I could fit all those people in my yard! I chose theTraditional Fourth Celebration and it couldn't have worked out better for us. But hey everyone's parties differ, so check out theGourmet, Kid Friendly, On the Go and Big Party Fourth Celebrations to see which one would fit your party style.


Sam's Club sent me everything I needed to make my party a success including food, plates, napkins and even Croquet! It was so great because I didn't have to cook anything but the beans since the Man does all the grilling around here! We went tubing the day of the party, so it got started a bit late the way it was. I was so glad that everything was already prepared and I just had to slice up a watermelon.

As you can see, it was already dark by the time we started eating. That sure didn't matter much though. Everyone loved the food and for once I couldn't really accept the compliment for making it. The potato salad was especially good and a heck of a lot cheaper than buying it from the grocer's deli.

Those hamburger patties you see down there were just frozen patties, but with a little bit of salt, pepper and seasoning salt they turned out awesome and everyone loved them!


Our party was a huge hit and honestly the best part of it for me was not having to make much. We had plenty of leftovers too! Check outwww3.samsclub.com/summer for party and menu ideas of of course some tasty recipes!

"Our family owned business is headed by Holly Eve, our founder and a design and candle artisan. Holly combined her two passions, helping those around her and her love for candles.

In early 2007, we met the candle maker whose candles Holly always admired. She talked with him about her design ideas and her vision as to how they would look on his unique and glowing candles. A friendship and partnership ensued. black & light® co. was born."

I am a candle fanatic. My house is adorned in them. I amnever without a candle. If we have a storm and the power goes out, I never worry. I have enough candles to keep our house lit for days. You'd think I'd be fine with that.

No, I'm not though. I look at the candle section of every store I go into. I smell them, I feel them. I just may be a candle addict. Hey, we all gotta have something, right?

Yes. Yes, we do!

I just can't help but be mesmerized by their glow and sometimes their scent. They're calming, romantic and pretty. There's nothing better than watching a scary movie with candles lit throughout the room. It totally intensifies the eeriness I think!



I was sent the 6" Dragonfly - Fern and it arrived ever so beautiful in a keepsake gift box. In fact, I still have the box. I like it that much. Oh this review isn't about the box?

Oops! It's pretty though, isn't it?



Ok, now on to the candle. Don't mind me, I get sidetracked too easily. First of all, I love the dragonflies all over it. They are just too cute! The candle looks great alone or on a cute little holder. Since it's already decorated, both options work great. They burn great and are pretty long lasting. I've burned for about 5 hours and have barely put a dent in the candle.

Black & Light offers a variety of lovely decorative candles. They even have ones with butterflies, which I was so tempted to get! Next time, for sure!
