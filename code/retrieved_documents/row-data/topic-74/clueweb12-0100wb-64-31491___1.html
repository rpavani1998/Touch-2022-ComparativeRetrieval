» Paris Via Eurostar Fully Escorted Day Tour (Standard Class)




Highlights:
*Hotel transfer from your Central London hotel * Guided panoramic tour of Paris * Fast entry tickets to the second level of the Eiffel Tower * Cruise on the River Seine * Guide to assist you throughout the day, including on board Eurostar * Optional visit to The Louvre (at additional cost)

There is simply no better way to visit Paris in a leisurely day out from London. This tour has proved to be extremely popular and great value!

After your free hotel transfer we connect you with your representative at St Pancreas International who will escort you onboard Eurostar and throughout the day in Paris. Travelling at speeds up to 186mph and passing en route through the 31 mile long channel tunnel you will be in Paris in just 2 hours 40 minutes. Upon arrival you will enjoy a panoramic tour of the city taking in all the major sites including Notre Dame, Champs Elysees, the River Seine and the Arc de Triomphe before arriving at the Eiffel Tower where we enjoy fast entry group access.









In the afternoon we take a cruise on the River Seine with spectacular views of the Notre Dame. After our cruise, if you so wish enjoy a guided tour of the Louvre* and see the world famous Mona Lisa. Alternatively, you can chose how to spend your free time.

In the early evening we head back to Gare du Nord for our return journey to London where you will be met and escorted back to your hotel making the tour completely fully escorted!

* Optional extras not included in the price - purchase from your guide on the day
(Option 1: Louvre entry & guided walking tour: 25 GBP, Option 2: Louvre entry without guided tour: 7 GBP)


IMPORTANT: Please note, this tour is non-refundable and non-amendable!

Pick up from your Central London hotel will be at 6.00 (AM).
We will return to London at around 9.15 (PM).
