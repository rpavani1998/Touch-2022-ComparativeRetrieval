Travel Options
Check out our optional sightseeing. Save time and money by pre-booking your sightseeing tours before you travel.

Jack the Ripper Evening Tour

Highlights: Retrace the steps of the worlds most famous serial killer - Jack the Ripper! Hear about the gruesome deaths that took place in Whitechapel at the end of the 19th century. The tour includes a drive through London's East end. Optional drink inside the Sherlock Holmes pub.
Duration: 3 hours
Departure: Departs from the Grosvenor Victoria Hotel at 7:00pm daily except Tuesday & Thursday - April through October and Wednesday, Friday & Sunday - November through March



Royal London Morning Tour

Highlights: Private Tour of the Tower of London before it opens to the public with yeoman warder. View Changing of the Guard at Buckingham Palace and panoramic tour with viewing of Houses of Parliament,Trafalgar Square and a Thames river cruise.
Duration: Approx 4 hours and 45 minutes.
Departure: April-October: Daily; November-March : Daily except Tuesday and Thursday; Closed Dec. 25, 26 & Jan 1; Departs 8:15am thru March 31, 2012; 8:00am April 1, 2012-March 31, 2013



Paradis Latin - Champagne Revue 9:30pm

Highlights: Enjoy the colorful revue "Paradis A la Folie" and a half bottle of champagne
at Paradis Latin.
Duration:approximately 1 hour and 45 minutes
Departures: Daily year round except for Tuesdays and special events





Illuminations of Paris by Minibus

Highlights: You will be escorted by our driver-guide for a minibus tour (about 1 1/2 hours) of Paris at night with all its attractions and monuments wonderfully lit. This tour includes the Pick-up and the Return at your hotel by minibus.
Duration: 1.5 hours
Departure: Nightly all year round
