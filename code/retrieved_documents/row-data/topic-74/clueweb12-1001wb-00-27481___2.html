Paris


 

The world is a better place thanks to the Ville Lumière.
No city embodies the spirit of the Enlightenment, as well as the pleasure of everyday life, more than Paris. Unsurpassed cultural activity has always put the city at the forefront of innovation, be it artistic, political or technological.

A new revolution is about to come to Paris: A rotating tower to grace the city's skyline, capable of living up to the city’s unique standards of sophistication.  Furthermore, 120 years after the Eiffel Tower revolutionized civil engineering, the Rotating Tower will again enlighten the way towards logical Architecture:  designed by engineering and paving the way to the Fourth Industrial Revolution.
