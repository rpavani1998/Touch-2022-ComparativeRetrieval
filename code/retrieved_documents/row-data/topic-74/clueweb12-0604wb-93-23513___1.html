Your time is split between Europe's two favourite capitals, London and Paris. Historic London is renowned for its pomp and pageantry as well as its reputation as a cultural trendsetter. As you'll soon see, Paris constantly delights the senses with its cuisine, precious art and its sheer elegance.

  • Paris Sightseeing includes ascent of the Eiffel Tower and views of Notre Dame Cathedral and the Champs Elysees.

Day Destination Description
Day 1 Depart USA DEPART USA: Ovenight flight to London.
Days 2-5 London LONDON (4 NIGHTS): Experience London - from the pomp and pageantry of the Royal Horse Guards to the welcoming pubs and bustling markets - this city has it all. Relax after check-in or spend time exploring the sights and sounds of London. On your first full day in London, your expert Local Guide takes you on a morning sightseeing tour including views of the Houses of Parliament, Big Ben, Westminster Abbey and Buckingham Palace. On your second full day enjoy an included visit to Windsor, with an included visit to the castle and time to shop. The rest of the time is yours to enjoy as you please. Stroll through Hyde Park, visit the dinosaurs at the Natural History Museum, have lunch in Covent Garden or explore Camden Markets. Shop in the West End, Bond Street and Knightsbridge, enjoy a top stage production or take a trip outside of the city to the pretty Cotswolds - your options are endless and your Local Host will be available to suggest activities or to help with organizing any excursions during your stay.
Thistle Euston
Days 6-8 Paris PARIS (3 NIGHTS) : Transfer to St. Pancras International Station in London and board the Eurostar, a high-speed train service, to Gare du Nord in central Paris. On arrival in the French capital, you are transferred to your hotel for three nights in this vibrant city. The next morning, enjoy a sightseeing tour of Paris which includes an ascent to the second level of the Eiffel Tower, a wonderful drive up the Champs Elysees to the Arc de Triomphe and views of Notre Dame Cathedral. Afterwards, you are free to do as you please. There is time to sample a patisserie, to shop in the chic French boutiques or to enjoy a visit to the Palace of Versailles or the Louvre - the choice is yours. This evening, why not take in a spectacular Parisian cabaret?
Mercure Tour Eiffel Suffren
Day 9 USA PARIS - USA: After breakfast, bid your new-found friends 'au revoir' as your city break comes to an end. Transfers to Charles De Gaulle airport are available. Conditions apply.
