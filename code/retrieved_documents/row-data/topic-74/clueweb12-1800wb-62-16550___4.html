Cafe de Paris


(3 Coventry Street, London W1D 6BL)


 

Café De Paris is all about dazzling glamour and opulence. Holding over 700 people, the venue contains three separate bars in addition to the mezzanine and main dance floor—the Red Bar, the Blue Bar and the Fantasy Rooms (VIP area).
