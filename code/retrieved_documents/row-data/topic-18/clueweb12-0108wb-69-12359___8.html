Full Description: The Horizon Smokers 16 Inch Classic Smoker Grill is the choice for thousands of backyard Barbecue enthusiasts because of its versatility and price. The Classic has also been recommended by Cooking Magazines and Television Personalities because of its Classic Look and Great Design. With its offset firebox and large cooking chamber it can easily cook for a small family or feed up to 40 people. The double damper system makes it easy to regulate your temperature. From a low 160 degrees for slow smoking to 250 degrees for barbecuing, all the way up to 325 for grilling, this smoker can do it all. The firebox dimensions are 16 diameter x 16 long and the cooking chamber dimensions are 16 diameter x 32 long. There is over 660 total square inches of cooking surface. The wheels are 12 inch diameter and the front shelf is 9 x 30. The 16 Inch Classic comes with two 15 x 15 chrome grates, a chrome firebox cooking grate, a firebox charcoal grate, and a clean out tool for to clean out ash from the cooking chamber and firebox. There is a 10 year warranty on the welding structure, and a lifetime on the burnout of the firebox.
