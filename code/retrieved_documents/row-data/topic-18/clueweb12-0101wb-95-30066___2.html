Gas Charcoal Grills



Gas charcoal grills are dual purpose grills that allow you to have the best of both worlds. Charcoal is preferred by many outdoor cooks for meat, but the separate gas grill allows you to cook all your side dishes right along with your grilled meats. No more running back and forth between the kitchen and patio to complete your meal. When all your kitchen helpers disappear, you can handle it all yourself with a dual purpose combination grill.

Gas charcoal grills come in different sizes. Some models are only marginally larger than an average grill. Others, such as the twin chamber charcoal and gas grills, are a bit larger. The larger models are excellent choices for a complete outdoor kitchen. They are perfect for beside the pool, at the lake house or any other spot where people like to gather. With a propane tank and charcoal, there is no need to run electrical or gas wiring from the house to the outdoor kitchen.

Gas charcoal grills can be found at many home improvement stores and on the Internet. Many models are not much more expensive than a regular quality charcoal grill. Be sure to select a quality model with good construction. You want to check for a tight sealing lid or door. Be sure that the grill racks will stay in place when you are working with food on them. Models with separate lids or doors for each side will help you keep the temperature regulated while working with food on either side.
