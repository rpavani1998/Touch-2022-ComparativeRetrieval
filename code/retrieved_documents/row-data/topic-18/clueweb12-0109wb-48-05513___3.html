High heat, table-top grilling
The Hibach grill must be used on a table top and is therefore messier than a stand-alone grill. However, it uses less fuel (real charcoal is best because it leaves less ash), starts quickly because the heat is contained, and its high heat is ideal for a perfect rare steak or seared hors d'oevres. The high heat also means cooking must be constantly monitored - not a problem at table top. It is solidly built which is a plus for table-top cooking and does not come with a cover because it is not appropriate for slow cooking. Pros: Cooks efficiently, Adds flavor to meal, Quick grilling
