Wood Pellet Grills - Royall 3000 Wood Pellet Grill

 

Wood Pellet Grill Description

The Royall 3000 wood pellet grill is a step up from our Royall 2000 wood pellet grill model. The Royall 3000 wood pellet grill adds additional grilling space. It is also a great, affordable, solution for Long - Slow - Wood Smoked Barbecue. It has all the essential features you will need to cook for your entire dinner party. Be careful because before you know it you'll be ordering Royall wood pellets to keep your 3000 humming along because nothing beats slow cooked 'Que.


Wood Pellet Grill Features

  • Cook for 8 People
  • Large enough for a whole turkey
  • Set it and forget it temperature control
  • Heavy steel construction holds the heat

Wood Pellet Grill Specifications

  • Cooking Area - 627 square inches (expandable)
  • Cooking Volume - 3900 cu. inches
  • Hopper Capacity - 40 pounds
  • Made in USA high density cartridge heater/igniter (automatically ignites your pellets)
  • High Quality combustion fan
  • 1 RPM Auger motor
  • Heavy Duty 14 gauge steel
  • Two Swivel Casters
  • 3-Speed Analog Temperature Controller
  • 20,000 Btu/hr max capacity of 425 degrees
  • 2.35 lbs/hr max consumption of pellets
  • Dimensions: 21.5" X 34.5" x 46.5" (DxWxH)
  • Shipping Weight - 180 lbs.
  • 5 Year Limited Warranty
  • MADE IN THE USA

Taste:

The food cooked on a Royall™ Wood Pellet Grill taste better than any gas or charcoal fired grill. Royall™ Wood Pellet Grills are guaranteed to increase your outdoor dining pleasure. A wood fire is simply a more natural and a tastier way to cook food. Just ask any professional chef.

Safety:
A Royall™ Wood Pellet Grill is safer to use than that of a gas or charcoal fired grill. It is impossible for explosions to occur with a Royall™ Wood Pellet Grill because we have no gas leaks and no hot coals. Royall™ uses safe non-toxic and ecological friendly wood pellets produced in the north country of Wisconsin.

Convenience:
Set it and forget it. Royall™ Wood Pellet Grills do not need to be monitored 100% of the time while BBQ. Unlike a gas or charcoal fired grill, you can walk away and not have to worry about your food getting to well done. The idea behind a Royall™ Wood Pellet Grill is to enjoy your company that is over and not have to worry about your grill.

Healthy:
When cooking your food, Royall™ Wood Pellet Grills do not add any additional cholesterol, fat or calories. This indirect cooking method produces even heat throughout the Royall™ Wood Pellet Grill which is better than gas or charcoal grills. In the end, Royall™ Wood Pellet Grills are better for your health.

Royall™ Smoker:
Royall™ Wood Pellet Grills are absolutely the best smokers on the market today. When cooking with Royall™ brand Wood Pellets, the smoke penetrates the meat to leave a wonderful smoke ring.


Flare-Ups:
The Royall™ Wood Pellet Grill has no open flames or gas to cause flare ups. It is almost impossible to burn your food. With the help of Royall™ Wood Pellet Grills, barbequing is not just for men anymore.

Environmentally Safe:
Royall™ wood pellets are made from 100% natural hardwoods with no oils added for the flavored wood pellets that are on the market. Royall™ wood pellets burn clean and are a renewable resource for our green carbon footprint business model.

Versatile:
A Royall™ Wood Pellet Grill is literally three appliances built into one conventional appliance. Royall™ Wood Pellet Grills will not only grill your food to perfection, but it will also bake and smoke all with the flip of a simple switch.

Economical:
Royall™ Wood Pellet Grills costs less to operate than most charcoal grills. One 20 lb. bag of Royall™ wood pellets will cook an average of five times more than a bag of charcoal and very little clean up. Ask yourself what you do with the ash of a charcoal grill when you are done cooking and how nice it would not to have to do that anymore.
