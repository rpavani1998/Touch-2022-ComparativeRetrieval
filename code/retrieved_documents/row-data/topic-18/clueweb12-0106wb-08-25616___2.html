Contemporary look
We bought this item as a center piece and table alternative around some wicker lounge chairs. We really like the size and look. The edge serves to hold the drinks or feet. It does clean really easy with a damp cloth and distributes some nice heat. However the only issue we have is the noise. Once it's running you can hear the hissing sound of the gas diverting throughout the rings. We even bought more lava rocks, but that didn't help. I was under the impression you can not hear the gas flow just like in a patio heater. Pros: Durable, Attractive Design, Easy To Assemble, Easy To Clean, Easy To Use
Cons: makes noise
