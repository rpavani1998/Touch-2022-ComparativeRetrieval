Online Courses

Designed to explore topics in depth, our Online Courses provide research-rich content to help provide early childhood educators the background they need to better understand and facilitate motor skill development in the classroom. Each course is self-paced and takes approximately 2 – 3 hours to complete. You will be able to print a certificate of completion for each course.
