Online Masters Degree in Nursing

Christine S. Baker
September 30th, 2010

A Master’s degree in nursing prepares you with the skills and advanced training necessary to deliver high-quality nursing care in a variety of settings within the health care environment. One of the most convenient ways to earn your Master’s in Nursing Degree (MSN) is through an accredited online nursing degree program. Accredited online Nursing Master’s degree programs are available from a growing number of accredited online nursing schools in an easy and convenient format designed with the working nurse in mind.

Background Info about Master’s in Nursing Care

Nurses who graduate with an MSN degree are called Advanced Practice Nurses. As an Advanced Practice Nurse, you will provide health care services that are commonly delivered by physicians, and you will focus on one of four advanced practice areas such as:

  • Clinical Nurse Specialist (CNS)
  • Certified Nurse Midwife (CNM)
  • Certified Nurse Anesthetist (CRNA)
  • Nurse Practitioner (NP)

MSN Salary and Employment Outlook

Nursing is the largest healthcare occupation in the Unites States. According to the U.S. Bureau of Labor Statistics (BLS), nurses earn an average of $52,330 annually. And with the baby boomers retiring daily and medical technology constantly evolving, an advanced healthcare degree is the best prescription for job opportunities and advancing your career.

Explore online master’s degrees in nursing from reputable accredited online colleges and universities:

  • South University – Online South University enjoys a long-standing tradition of excellence in education and personalized student attention. Enjoy the convenience of earning yourbachelor’s degree entirely online.
  • Kaplan University School of Nursing – When you enroll in Kaplan University School of Nursing online, you will enjoy the flexibility of pursuing your educational goals from the convenience of your own home or anywhere you have Internet access.
  • Walden University – You understand the needs of your patients and your organization—but you see the opportunities for improvement in the nursing profession. With Walden University’s online degree programs in nursing, you can become an agent of change and a leader in your field.
  • Capella University – Online Capella University provides working adults the opportunity to earn online degrees in a variety of demanding fields. As an accredited university, Capella delivers a challenging education to more than 23,000 students.

Nurses continue to enjoy thriving employment growth opportunities and benefits. If an MSN degree is your goal, now is the perfect time to start your nursing education online and prepare yourself for an incredibly rewarding career.

Online Masters Degree in Criminal Justice

Christine S. Baker
February 24th, 2010


Contrary to what many may think, the field of criminal justice is not just restricted to policing duties. In fact, criminal justice is one of the most diverse professions pursued by online students. The field of criminal justice spans areas such as corrections, education, law enforcement, security, research and the legal profession. In addition, within each of these professions there are countless attractive and exciting sub-categories that attract online students.
While a bachelors’ degree will afford you middle management positions in the field of criminal justice, it is after the successful pursuit of anonline masters’ degree in criminal justice before one can make a significant impression in the field of criminal justice.

Background of the Masters in Criminal Justice

The online masters’ in criminal justice is an intense study of the concepts and framework of make up the field of criminal justice. It employs high levels of research that ensure that students are kept at the forefront of technologies and information related to the field of criminal justice. This research involves studies of problems affecting the effectiveness of criminal justice concepts and how they can be improved. Researches may include efforts through policy adjustments, financial and social investment. By the end of the online master in criminal justice, students will be able to identify trends in the areas of corrections, policing, criminology and criminal justice. In addition, they will be able to employ the leadership skills gained during the program to effect change within the criminal justice system.

Curriculum of the Online Masters Degree in Criminal Justice

The curriculum of the online master in criminal justice is broken up into several sections that enhance the ease in which the material is delivered. Each segment is available online from theonline university’s database, where students can access them at any time to their convenience. Most online master degree programs are broken into segments of:

  • Core Theory: This features studies in the theory, philosophy, law enforcement and practices of criminal justice. It is supported by several online seminars that focus on areas of law and social control, criminal justice and criminology.
  • Administration and Policy: This exposes students to the management side of criminal justice. It features subjects such as criminal justice management, policy analysis and the juvenile justice system.
  • Special Topics in Criminal Justice: These are many electives that students take to widen their knowledge in the field of criminal justice. These electives include subject areas such as white-collar crime, police effectiveness, correction and rehabilitation, community relation and crime.
  • Research: All students are required to do a research thesis supported by substantial evidence and data that they must defend before a graduation board.

Top online Colleges for Online Masters Degree in Criminal Justice

Some of the top online colleges for the pursuit of a masters’ degree in criminal justice include:

  • Kaplan University Online
  • University of Maryland
  • Columbia Southern University

Employment Opportunities for Online Masters Criminal Justice Graduates

Many organizations utilize the services of criminal justice professionals. These range from both government and non-governmental organizations. Graduates of theonline masters in criminal justice assume management duties in areas such as the secret services, border control, bureau of Alcohol, Tobacco and Firearms and the FBI.

Study Forensic Psychology Degrees Online

Christine S. Baker
December 31st, 2009


Due to the influence of television, many people confuse forensic science with Forensic Psychology. Although both are intertwined, Forensic Psychologists focus mainly on psychological issues and their relationship to the justice system. They focus on legal issues, public policies, laws, competency and the state of mind of offenders when they commit crime.Forensic Psychologists play a vital role in determining the type of punishment handed down to offenders. Forensic Psychologists impart their expert evaluation to judges through close consultation before they issue sentences.

Education Required for Studying Forensic Psychology Degrees Online

Forensic Psychology degrees online entry requirements may vary from one university to another. However, universities offeringForensic Psychology degrees online require applicants to have a 3.5 GPA score or higher in addition to having successfully completed a Bachelor of Science degree in a related field such as Anthropology, Chemical Engineering, Genetics, Biology or Human Biology. Studying Forensic Psychology degree online provide you with the opportunity to secure the requisite qualifications and abilities needed to cement your career in this dynamic and intriguing field.

Education Required to Work as a Forensic Psychologist

A Masters in Psychology with specialization in a sub-field is the minimum requirement needed to practice within the field. APhD Degree in Forensic Psychology or a related field is more highly regarded.

Is a Career in Forensic Psychology Right For Me?

If you are interested in playing an active role in law enforcement, the field of Forensic Psychology has many sub-fields in which you can establish a career. Forensic Psychologists specialize in areas such as clinical psychology, criminal investigative, counseling or cognitive psychology. In addition to the academic requirements, your role as a Forensic Psychologistwill require possessing certain traits to facilitate and shape your success in the field.


The job requires working closely with other professionals such as lawyers, medical professionals, investigators, as well as with criminals. Because it requires you to work with so many different people, people skills are of vital importance. The job also requiresForensic Psychologists to be resourceful and the ability to exhibit patience as well as commitment. Therefore, before deciding on a career in Forensic Psychology, it is very important that you consider if you are willing to invest the time and money required to obtain the appropriate qualifications as well as what area you would like to specialize in.

Types of Forensic Psychology Degrees Online

Master of Arts in Forensic Psychology – A Master’s in Forensic Psychology targets individuals seeking to enter into the field as well as functioning professionals in field wishing to upgrade their skills. The curriculum design of the program focuses heavily on topics that teach the theory and practices established by the discipline such as psychology and criminal justice and how they can be applied to the criminal justice system. TheMaster’s in Forensic Psychology degree program allows for specialization in areas such as Forensic Psychology for Application to Law Enforcement, Program Planning and Evaluation in Forensic Settings, Legal and Organizational Consultation and Psychology and Legal Systems.

Forensic Psychology Doctoral Degrees Online

The PhD in Forensic Psychology provides an in-depth analysis of systems that can be implemented to make significant contributions to the field of Forensic Psychology. The study allows participants to specialize in various areas of specialization such as Psychology and Treatment of Juveniles, Crisis Intervention, Substance Abuse Assessment, and Psychology of Cultural Differences. All students must conduct extensive research in their area of specialization and write a dissertation that they will defend before a dissertation committee.

Forensic Psychology Careers

Forensic Psychologists work in a variety of settings within the civil legal and criminal justice system such as community health centers, prisons, court service units as well as working as private consultants.

What to Look for in an Online Masters Degree Program?

Christine S. Baker
December 11th, 2009



With today’s tough job market, many people are returning to school to earn higher degrees. Most employers demand prospective candidates have advanced degrees in addition to other qualifications. According to the Institute of Education Sciences, throughout the past 25 years, the number of students pursuing Master’s degrees has almost doubled. More students are entering graduate programs to get the credentials to compete for increasingly popular jobs and a bigger paycheck. Wouldn’t you like to have a great salary and a fulfilling career?

Is an Online Masters Degree Worth The Cost?

Anytime you choose to advance your education, it is a good idea to decide whether it should be online or on-campus. Earning anonline Master’s degree requires commitment and a fair amount of resources, so choosing the right program makes all the difference in the world. Studies have shown that people with advanced degrees are at an advantage for career opportunities, advancements and higher salaries.

Why Earn a Master’s Degree Online?

Before you make a commitment to enrolling in an online Master’s degree program, you should know why you want to earn a Master’s degree and if it will help you to achieve your career goals. Research has shown that even though an advanced degree is attractive, it is still not the deciding factor for the ideal job opportunity. What really matters in the end is your experience, qualifications, talent, drive, ambition and a bit of luck.

Are All Online Institution Credible and Legitimate?

Most online institutions will advertise that they offer the best degrees at the lowest cost to draw you in, however not all institutions are legitimate. During your search for anonline Master’s degree program, it is important to seek an online institution that has accreditation from a reputable organization. Look for institutions that have been accredited by a regionally or nationally recognized accrediting agency with the U.S. Department of Education. To find out if the online institution is accredited by a nationally or regional accrediting agency you can access the U.S. Department of Education search engine athttp://ope.ed.govaccrediationsearc.asp.

Beware of Diploma Mills

Diploma mills are everywhere! These faux schools make promises and provide fraudulent, worthless degrees with no educational value and can lead you to career and financial ruin. In the real world, these degrees will not help your career or lead to success in the competitive job market.

You also want to find out if your online Master’s degree program is 100% online as well as if it provides round the clock technical support and offers all the benefits to earn your online degree with ease.

Types of Master Degrees Online

There are a variety of Master’s degree program online . Some of the most popular online degrees are Master of Science (MS), Master of Arts (MA), Master of Business Administration (MBA), Master of Education (M Ed), Master of Health Administration (MHA) and Master of Fine Arts.

Paying for Online Master’s Degree Program

Online students have a variety of financial aid options to assist with the rising cost of college expenses. There are federal grants and loans available, in addition to private scholarships and other outside lending sources. Earning anonline Master’s degree can be expensive and challenging, but with proper research and adequate planning, you will be able to have a wonderful online experience.

Special Education Degrees Online

Christine S. Baker
October 12th, 2009


Special education entails using individualized and systematically monitored teaching procedures, adapted materials and techniques and other interventions designed to provide additional services and support to help learners with special needs achieve their particular educational goals. The nature of this field will require specially trained educators to help these students learn and the ability to identify their special needs whether physical, social or educational.

Requirements to Become Online Special Education Teacher

To become a special education teacher, students must complete a degree program in special education along a with a teacher education certificate.

Online Bachelor Degrees in Special Education

A Bachelor degree program in special education typically takes four years (depending on the number of courses you take) to complete and will require a high school diploma or GED. The program provides teaching in general education that includesonline courses in foundations of teaching, interdisciplinary studies, effective teaching practices for special education and instructional teaching. Students will undergo supervised practice teaching (in a special school for students with disabilities) and completion of a professional portfolio. Students who successfully complete the program will have acquired the effective teaching skills needed to teach learners with mild to moderate disabilities. The program leads to licensure in special education.

Master’s Degree in Special Education

The Master of education degree in special education caters specifically to special education teachers with teacher certification who wish to advance their studies in the field of special education. Participants of the Masters inspecial education degree program will acquire specialized training and in-depth instructional courses from experienced professionals within their respected fields. These courses will cover areas of learning disabilities, mental retardation, emotional and physical disabilities. Participants must have access to a class of children with special needs so they may be able to complete all programmed assignments given during the course.

Employment Outlook for Special Education Teachers

Special educators can obtain employment in public and private school systems, counseling centers, mental facilities, military, private learning centers and places of safety. The type ofjob offers received will depend on your level of qualification and area of specialization.

Schools that offer Special Education Degrees Online

Capella University
Ellis University Online
Western Governors University
Walden University

Salary Range for Special Education Teachers

The salary for special education teachers varies widely depending on the level of training and experience, area of specialization and source of employment.Special education teachers working in a preschool, kindergarten or elementary schools can earn up to $45,000 per year. Those teaching in private institutions or corporations can earn over $60,000 per year, PhD holders within the field can earn up $100,000 per year.
