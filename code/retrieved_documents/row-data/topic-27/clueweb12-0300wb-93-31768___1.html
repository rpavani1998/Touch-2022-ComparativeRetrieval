Realistic Flame-Effect Gas Stoves

Stoves Are Us have a huge choice of gas stoves from the leading manufacturers, including Aarrow stoves, Dovre, Esse, Hunter, Broseley, Gazco, Villager and Yeoman. Our gas stoves feature either, coal-effect, log-effect, or wood flame-effects in a traditional cast-iron stove body. Gas stoves provide a stunning focal point to any room, reflecting the comforting glow of real stoves.

Although conventional flue gas stoves require an existing chimney, or twinwall system, there are also flueless gas stoves available which do not require chimneys. Some of our gas stoves are available with an LPG convertor kit, helping to increase the savings on your fuel-bill. For more information on a gas stove, or for expert advice from our qualified team, please contact 01484 434321.

All our gas stove prices include VAT, delivery to the mainland UK and a selection of free offers.
