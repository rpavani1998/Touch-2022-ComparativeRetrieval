Exmoor Electric Stove

Complete operation by remote control

The latest in the range is the electric version of the ever popular Exmoor stove. Available in traditional Matt Black, this stove perfectly combines the classic good looks of a woodburner with the ease of an electric stove. At its heart is the unique VeriFlame™ technology which allows you to choose from 3 brightness levels and blue or orange flame setting to match your mood exactly – and everything is operated from remote control to save you bending over!
