Articles About Traditional Finnish Saunas

 

Many sauna enthusiasts believe there is no better sauna bath than a traditional Finnish sauna bath. For them, the hot air temperature generated by the sauna heater of their choosing, the loyly or vapor that comes from tossing water on the sauna rocks, and the feel of the vihta striking their skin all help to define the authentic hot sauna experience.

Traditional Finnish saunas are available in different sizes and styles to suit different needs; some folks may elect to build a large, multi-bather cabin outdoors with a pre-cut sauna kit, while others may prefer a more private, one-person, modular indoor sauna room.

The following articles cover a variety of topics related to Finnish saunas, such as sauna stoves, sauna rocks, sauna accessories, and sauna bathing tips.    

Finnish Saunas – Providing High Times and High Temperatures
No one understands saunas better than the people of Finland. Even though the sauna was not invented in Finland, its importance to Finnish culture and society cannot be overemphasized. The motivating factors for the Finns’ steadfast devotion to sauna use are varied, but almost universally shared by the Finnish people is the conviction that sauna baths benefit human health, hygiene and well being.

Home Saunas – Which Type Is Right for You?
Before you buy a sauna, make sure that you know your options. Which type of sauna will best suit your lifestyle and fulfill your personal needs: a prefabricated sauna, a pre-cut sauna kit, a traditional Finnish sauna, or a far infrared heat therapy room?

Pre-cut Sauna Kits Make Great Saunas
Great Saunas knows just how popular do-it-yourself projects can be with people who enjoy working with their hands. We also realize that not everyone has the time or patience for tasks that are overly complicated. That’s why we make it a point to ensure that our pre-cut sauna kits can be assembled quickly and easily while still providing the satisfaction that comes with tackling an endeavor that’s just enough of a challenge to be fun.

Sauna Accessories for an Even Better Sauna Bath
Experienced sauna bathers know just how valuable the right sauna accessories can be. Often, it is these accoutrements that distinguish a sauna session that is truly superior from one that is merely second-rate. Used properly, sauna accessories can help a sauna bather create an ideal ambience for a decidedly delectable dip in the soothing heat of a sauna.

Sauna Rocks – Another Hot Sauna Topic
Even in the modern era of far infrared sauna therapy, many sauna enthusiasts hold firm to their belief that sauna rocks are essential to achieving the definitive hot sauna experience. While sauna fans are forever at liberty to debate what defines an authentic sauna bath, it is an absolute must that rock sauna owners always use proper sauna rocks in their saunas.

Sauna Rooms – For Greater Health, Homes and Happiness
For its proven positive influence on human health and well being, an indoor sauna room should be considered an absolute must for virtually every person who owns a home. Many health benefits have been attributed to regular sauna bathing, including improved blood circulation and strengthened immunity to illness.

Saunas and Sauna Kits – Choices for Bathers and Builders
If you’re interested in buying or building a sauna for your personal use, you must first decide which type of sauna would best fulfill your needs and accommodate your lifestyle. Being able to differentiate between the many sauna types should be the initial goal of every inexperienced sauna buyer and prospective sauna builder.

Sauna Stoves – Wood Burning, Electric or Gas?
Which type of sauna heater or sauna stove is best is truly a matter of opinion. One type may be better than another in certain conditions and circumstances, but the bottom line seems to be that electric sauna heaters, gas sauna heaters, and wood burning sauna heaters each have unique appeal.

The Finnish Sauna – 16 Steps to Total Pleasure
There are many guidelines but few, if any, fixed rules to deriving the most pleasure and fulfillment from a sauna bath, as everyone is free to blaze their own trail to sauna satisfaction. This article offers some proven pointers to head you in the right direction.

The Sauna – Your Best Setting for Sweating
Sweating is your body’s way of regulating its temperature and removing waste material. In the interest of better health, your aim should not be to block, inhibit or reduce perspiration; it should be to sweat more effectively, preferably in a controllable environment far from any risk of embarrassment or threat of rejection. What better environment exists for achieving such a goal than a home sauna?

The Traditional Sauna – A Finnish Vocabulary Lesson
Among the wonderful benefits a traditional Finnish sauna offers is a thorough cleansing of the body. Regular sauna use is also good for blood circulation, pain relief, and strengthening the immune system. The traditional Finnish sauna can do all that and much more, including serve as a magnificent introduction to Finnish vocabulary.
