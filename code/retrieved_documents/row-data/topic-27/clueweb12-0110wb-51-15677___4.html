284 Route 100

Dover , VT

$539,000 | 13 Bedrooms | 14 Bathrooms | MLS# 4044698

Listed by: Caplan, Richard/Deerfield Valley Real Estate

The Doveberry a classic New England country Inn. Currently being used as an Bed and Breakfast but could also be used as large home for a social group, ski club or multiple families. Located only one mile from Mount Snow ski area with convenient access right on Route 100 this property offers lots of room for the year round enjoyment of the Deerfield Valley and all it has from skiing and snowmobiling too hiking, biking, fishing, boating, motorcycle touring and more. The possibilities are vast with features to include a commercial galley kitchen with a walk-in refrigerator, Garland six burner oven with grill and griddle, pantry, wine storage and more. The dinning room seats 26 and there is a cozy living room with a wood burning buck stove and a full bar. Outside features parking for 26 cars, a one car garage, storage shed, and grassy lawn out back. In front is a decked walk way to an entry with wood stove and large storage closet. The walkout lower level has a two bedroom apt. with office
