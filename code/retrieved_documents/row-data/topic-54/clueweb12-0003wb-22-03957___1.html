Hydrangea Shrubs


Hydrangea shrubs are flowering shrubs that grow and bloom the best in morning sun with afternoon shade. Don't expect hydrangea plants to do well planted in heavy shade. The Pee Gee Hydrangea variety is one of the few hydrangea bushes hardy enough to grow in full sun.

A white flowering hydrangea will not change bloom color . However, the soil pH will cause somehydrangea shrubs to bloom pink or can be altered to change the bloom color to blue. Our planting instructions for hydrangea shrubs explains the color change as well as thecorrect time to prune your flowering shrubs.

Greenwood Nursery offers a selection of the best and hardiest hydrangea plants with some being Proven Winners  Hydrangeas varieties.
