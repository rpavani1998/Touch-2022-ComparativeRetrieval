Shrubs

Looking for shrubs? You will find your perfect garden plants for accenting, foundation plantings or  privacy hedges here at our online garden store.

Greenwood offers a wide variety of bare root shrubs available for your landscaping projects. Flowering shrubs make a colorful seasonal statement while evergreen shrubs remain the basic foundation shrubs and act as backdrops for perennials, ornamental grasses and other low growing shrubs.

You will also find large shrubs and bushes as well as a selection of shrubs for shade. Check out our fast growing shrubs for privacy hedges. Greenwood's Online Nursery, also, offers a great selection ofsmall shrubs for bordering or edging gardens or large landscaped areas.

Use the Greenwood Navigation Plant Finder to narrow down your shrubs selection for your garden ideas.


Bloom-A-Thon® Azaleas Beautiful in borders or clusters, the Bloom-A-Thon® Azaleas are evergreen flowering shrubs that will bloom in spring only to bloom again mid to late summer until fall. Sizes of the Bloom-A-Thon® Azalea flowering shrubs vary with each color.
Crape Myrtle Trees Crape myrtle trees are a favorite in the Southern garden because these heat tolerant ornamental trees that bloom profusely even during the most intense, humid summer heat when little else is in full bloom. Check Greenwood Nursery for more small trees
Evergreen Shrubs As hedges and foundation plantings, you will find evergreen shrubs provide privacy and clearly define spaces. Choose from our evergreen plant varieties to create your very own private outdoor room away from the house and other outdoor activities.

Flowering Shrubs Flowering shrubs are used as foundation plants, for privacy hedges or in fragrant gardens. Many of our best selling flowering shrub plants are commonly found in fast growing sun gardens where they attract butterflies and hummingbirds.

Top 9 reasons why shrubs are a great choice for any garden:

  • Shrubs are virtually maintenance free. 
  • Our living fence shrubs help you to maintain your privacy.
  • Several specimens of the same variety can be planted in groupings for a stronger visual effect.
  • Different varieties work well together as companion plantings  for diversity and harmony of form, foliage or flower.
  • They give a sense of volume in a flowerbed and can be used to set off garden accessoriessuch as statues, birdbaths, fountains, sculptures, and ornamental rocks.
  • Large shrubs can also be used as privacy hedges to create an intimate corner or as a backdrop for a flowerbed.
  • Many flowering shrubs attract humming birds, bees and butterflies.
  • Use evergreen shrubs as your foundation plantings to provide year around color and texture.
  • To hide an unsightly area - select from our living fence shrubs .
