    Read how the adidas adiZero 5-Star was designed!

    Too light to be stopped: adidas adiZero 5-Star. This extreme speed cleat supplies you with lightweight strength and stability as you make your way through the game, fast! The upper uses a single layer of SprintSkin. This synthetic material, though lightweight, is extremely strong with the help of the internal TPU support bands. Not only do they add strength, they increase stability and support for quick cuts, jukes and acceleration. A TPU bottom frame reinforces the upper by providing an extra layer of durability, while the heavy-duty toe box offers added protection. The non-removable, molded PU insole cradles your foot in long-lasting comfort as you make play after play. Sprint frame construction on the outsole uses geometrical learning to offer the perfect balance between speed and stability. A TraXion® stud configuration provides extreme acceleration and multidirectional traction on a variety of surfaces. The heel cup locks your foot in place as you take the defense for the ride of their lives.There's light and then there is the adidas adiZero 5-Star. Wt. 6.9 oz.
