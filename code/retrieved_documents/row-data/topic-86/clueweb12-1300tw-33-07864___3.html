Nike Wardour Chukka – Nike Going Casual?

Nike Wardour Chukka – Nike Going Casual?
Posted by @Asilves3, Yesterday View Comments


Since its existence Nike has always been known for their incredible contribution to the world of sports. Whether it be in the form of performance sneakers, performance sportswear, or other innovations in human performance, Nike has always concentrated on dominating the world of sports. Within the past year Nike’s casual division has been pushing out a whole bunch of sneaker releases geared more toward a casual look and feel. Check out one of Nike’s more recent causal releases the Nike Wardour Chukka. Are you excited to see to Nike delving in the casual sneaker market or should they stick to athletics?
