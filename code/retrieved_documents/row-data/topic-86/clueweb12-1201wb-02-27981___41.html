Druid’s Keep

March 6, 2011

I had just opened up my email and found a sad bit of news sitting in my inbox. Druid’s Keep in Magallanes is closing down.

 

March 12 is the last day of operations for Druids Keep Magallanes.  It has been a great 6 years plus, but, it’s time to move on.  Should you have questions on how to continue getting items from DK.  Please DO NOT hesitate to email me and ask how we can be of service to you.

In the meantime, from March 8 to March 12, DK will be on sale.

Thank you to all of DK’s friends and we will still continue seeing each other.

Regards,
Felix Cua,
Charles Tan
& Eduard Erese

I am saddened by this, seeing one of the best comic book shops coming to a close. I guess the industry here just isn’t as strong as I had hoped.

We at Comicology wish Felix and friends the best of luck in their future endeavors.
