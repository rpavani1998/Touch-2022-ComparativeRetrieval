You’re Buying WOT? BMW 640i Gran Coupe, Mercedes-Benz CLS550, Audi A7, or Jaguar XF

Written by: Zach Gale G+ on February 9 2012 6:00 PM

 




When you desire a luxury car but want more exclusivity and style, the four-door coupes four-doors with coupe-like profiles segment awaits. In the recently created class, theMercedes-Benz CLS-Class is now joined by the Audi A7, BMW 640i Gran Coupe, and Jaguar XF if you discount the more affordable Volkswagen CC and Hyundai Sonata.

With basic pricing information released on the BMW 640i Gran Coupe, we thought it was time to give this segment another look. Like the Mercedes-Benz CLS and Jaguar XF, BMW approaches this segment with a sedan bodystyle, unlike the Audi A7 hatchback. What brings together these four entries are a focus on exterior style, interior craftsmanship, and a price that assures you won’t be seeing 10 of them at the local Whole Foods market. Let’s take a look at what $80,000 will buy you at your local BMW, Mercedes-Benz, Audi, and Jaguar dealerships.

2013 BMW 640i Gran Coupe – $76,895 base price – The 640i Gran Coupe’s near-$80,000 starting price surprisingly places this BMW in the same price range as the V-8 powered Mercedes-Benz CLS550. The 640i Gran Coupe is powered by a 315-hp turbocharged inline six-cylinder engine.

 

2012 Mercedes-Benz CLS550 – $72,175 base price – Thanks to a low price compared to the BMW, an $80,000 budget actually allows for some perusing of Mercedes-Benz’s long options list. Why not indulge in the CLS550′s available full LED headlights?

 

 

2012 Audi A7 Prestige – $66,455 base price – Even the top trim 2012 Audi A7 trim level costs less than $70,000. That lower base price opens up possibilities like the Audi A7′s optional $5900 1300-watt sound system and still have a few dollars left over.

 

 

2012 Jaguar XF Supercharged – $68,975  base price – Though not as purpose-built for this class as the others, the newly refreshed 2012 Jaguar XF has plenty of style and, when supercharged, delivers 470 hp from its V-8 and a 0-60 mph time of under 5 seconds. Plus, unlike the BMW 640i Gran Coupe and Audi A7, the Jaguar XF is only offered in the U.S. with V-8 powerplants.

If you were ready to buy an $80,000 premium coupe-like sedan, would you choose a BMW 640i Gran Coupe, Audi A7, Mercedes-Benz CLS550, Jaguar XF, or look elsewhere? Tell us what you’d choose in the comments section below.
