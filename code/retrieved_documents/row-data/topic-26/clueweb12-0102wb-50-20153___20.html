Thread of the Day: Would You Stay With the Same Brand For Your Next Car?

Written by: Edward A. Sanchez G+ on February 9 2012 5:00 PM

 




Car owners tend to fall into two camps: Life-long loyalists, and fickle dilettantes that flit from brand to brand chasing the latest trend or hot model. Forum memberwestys wants to know if based on the experience with your current vehicle, if you would buy another from the same brand. An admitted VW loyalist, westys looks forward to buying aJetta TDI Sportwagen.

Others commenting on the thread have mixed ownership experiences. For some, their favorite brand is discontinued. Some prefer not to limit themselves, and take advantage of the variety of new models on the marketplace. Others have strict guidelines and conditions for their purchase consideration, such as available engines and transmissions (i.e. six-cylinder, manual transmission only), while others are dictated by practical needs (growing family, occupation.)

What do you drive now, and based on your experience with it, would you buy another from the same brand? Share your thoughts and experiences on today’s Thread of the Day!
