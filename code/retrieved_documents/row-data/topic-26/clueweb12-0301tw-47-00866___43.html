Thread of the Day: If You Had to Choose Just One Car, What Would It Be?

Written by: Christian Seabaugh [G+] on February 23 2012 5:00 PM

 




Earlier this week we played Desert Island in our Thread of the Day, and asked you to pick five cars – three cars for you, one for your significant other, and one for your teenager – that you’d want to be trapped on a desert island for all of eternity with.  Today’s Thread of the Day is a play on that theme.

For Today’s Thread of the Day, Frisky_Dingo asks the question, “If you had to choose justone vehicle to own for the rest of your life, to be used for every purpose and driving need, what would it be?” Here are the rules:

  1. It has to be a factory-offered production vehicle, so concepts, prototypes, and modified vehicles (likeFrisky_Dingo’s “G37 with a GT-R driveline” example) don’t count.
  2. Cost is no object.
  3. The car can be new or old, it doesn’t matter; keep in mind it will be your only car, for the rest of your life and maintaining something like a vintage Jaguar E-Type might be troublesome.

Before we turn things over to you, a couple things to keep in mind – this will be theonly car you will ever have for the rest of your life. AsFrisky_Dingo said, “If you pick a Veyron now, and five years down the road have a family, you’re walking everywhere, or cramming them in the luggage compartment.” With that in mind initial responses from the staff include theCadillac CTS-V Wagon, Audi RS4 Avant, and Porsche Panamera Turbo S – all practical, yet fun. Enough about us though, have at it in the comments below.
