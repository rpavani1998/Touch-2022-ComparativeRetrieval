If you are hearing a groaning sound coming from your car’s front end, be sure to check your wheel bearings immediately. Its likely that the noise is coming from a bad wheel bearing. This project will take a little time to remove and replace, but the bearing is pretty cost effective. It is however very important to check and replace your wheel bearing right away to prevent damage to other components. This is a MAJOR safety component of your car. The wheel bearings on you car sit between the axle and the wheel hub and allow your wheels to spin freely. These bearings carry the weight of your car, and their must be no slop or movement side to side or they can fail rapidly. Bad wheel bearings will adversely affect your steering, braking, and fuel economy.
