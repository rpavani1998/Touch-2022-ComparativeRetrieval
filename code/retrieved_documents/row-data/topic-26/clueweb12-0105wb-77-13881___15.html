Car Donation to A Safe Place

Donate Car to A Safe Place

Donation Line proudly accepts all vehicle donations for A Safe Place. We have towing agents in all areas of the United States ready to pickup your car donation ASAP. Non-Running, junk and salvage cars are also accepted. In addition to auto donations we accept donations of boats, trucks, RVs, motorcycles, jet skis, snowmobiles, planes and real estate.

A Safe Place

Mission Statement

To break the cycle of abuse by providing crisis intervention, support services, education and advocacy to survivors, their families and the community.

Car Donation to Charity

Simply complete our Online Vehicle Donation Form or call us Toll-free at1-877-227-7487 anytime, seven days a week to start our no cost, no hassle car donation process. Phones are answered by our experienced and trained operators. You will be contacted by our towing agent who will arrange a speedy pickup. The towing agent will provide a pickup receipt and the charity you select will send you a tax deduction letter.
