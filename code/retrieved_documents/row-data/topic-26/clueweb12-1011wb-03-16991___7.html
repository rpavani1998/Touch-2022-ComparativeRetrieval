Comparison Test: 2010 Audi S4 vs. BMW 335i

By Ed Hellwig | October 13, 2009


Comments (16)


Few cars have rules their class like the BMW 3 Series. There have been countless challengers over the years, yet they always fell short in one way or another.

No one has tried harder to break the stranglehold than Audi. It's A4 had been getting closer with every redesign and refresh, but was never quite there. The latest challenger is the 2010 S4, a newly supercharged, all-wheel drive sedan that has been realigned to better match up with BMW top 3 Series four-door.

So has Audi finally cracked this code? see for yourself in our latest comparison test.
