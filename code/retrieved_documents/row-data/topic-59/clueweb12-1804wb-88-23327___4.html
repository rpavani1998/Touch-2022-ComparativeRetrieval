Skiing

Cross Country Skiing The high scenic trails that are hiked in the summer are used by cross country skiers in the winter. Sprague Lake and Bear Lake areas in the National Park are the most popular. Shops in town offer lessons, guided tours and rental skis.

Downhill Skiing Drive an hour south on the Peak to Peak National Scenic Byway to Eldora Ski Resort to find a full-service ski resort with ski rentals, lessons, groomed slopes and lighting for some night-time skiing. There are several other Colorado ski areas within a few hours of Estes Park.

Eldora Ski Area
303-440-8700 | Located just 21 miles west of Boulder

Eldora is skiing, snowboarding, cross-country and snowshoe friendly!

Eldora opened for skiing in 1962.

Eldora receives 300 inches of snow per year.

Eldora boasts Colorado's best snowmaking system. Coverage is 100% of groomed terrain, more than any other resort in Colorado!

The base area of Eldora has an elevation of 9,200 feet above sea level. Top elevation is 10,800 feet.

The most difficult mountain to ski at Eldora is the Corona Bowl - a double black diamond!

Eldora is only 21 miles from Boulder and 47 miles from Denver.

Eldora has 680 acres of skiable terrain.

Eldora's longest run is 3 miles long!
