Mountain Guide Adventure

"This website was born from my desire to share with others, my passion for the mountains and all things vertical"

Sébastien Laurent

You will find everything here to feed your dreams of getting to the top of a summit, along with the means to realize them !

All our guides are High Mountain Guides, members of the UIAGM (The International Mountain Guide Associations Union).

This state certificate confer to the holder the highest level of international competence for the supervision of winter climbing, ice climbing, off-piste glacial skiing, rock climbing, via-ferrata, and mountain expeditions world wide.

This gives you a guarantee of quality supervision, which places your safety first.

Schedules & Activities

Cross-country Skiing  If you are looking for virgin snow, if you like spending nights up in mountain huts and enjoy traversing whole mountain ranges on skis, ski touring is for you.

The effort of ski touring uphill is more than compensated by your arrival at a mountain pass or summit. Untouched slopes are waiting for you to ski down…

Choose a tour to suit you : From the famous Chamonix-Zermatt Haute Route to a week of discovering new terrain in the Queyras.Heli Skiing/Snowboarding  Have you an insatiable appetite for virgin powder in the mountains ?

Then try heliskiing

This opens up 3700 metres of possible descent ( 2 helidrops), in a breathtaking setting…

We offer heliskiing in Italy, in the val Veny valley just 30 minutes from Chamonix.Ice Climbing  With the start of winter, a world comes to life which lies dormant for nine months of the year.
When the big freeze hits, the ice world comes to life for a fleeting instant.
The water and the cold sculpt incredible forms, and create the ice giants that become the climbers domain.....

...With the first glimmer of spring the narrow streams return, but for now, they are ours to conquer. With ice axes, crampons, dry tools and not a little innovation, the ascent of the icy monsters is an incredible feeling.

Come and have a taste of the ever changing world to experience something completely new.Mountaineering  Summer mountaineering offers multiple possibilities. Once the mountains have lost their blanket of ice and snow, the rock underneath becomes more accessible.

Ideal conditions allow you to accomplish your mountaineering dreams.
Whether you would like to climb La Meije, Mont Blanc or the Aiguille du Midi, now is the time to go for it. We offer guided services to some of the most beautiful summits in the Alps. Some are more arduous than others but none are trivial.

Your experience will grow with each summit climbed and peaks that seemed impossible before will be within your reach.

Therefore, each of our courses is based on a mutual exchange of experience and of course training since one is not born an alpinist, one becomes oneRock Climbing  Climbing vertical rock is like performing in a carefully choreographed dance.

Each route is like a mystery that must be unraveled. As the difficulty increases, the rock becomes less forgiving and one must find the exact sequences of movements in order to reach the top.


Whatever your ability level is, we will adapt our courses in order to best help you achieve your full potential.

We strive to teach you the requisite skills in order to make your climbing adventure safe.
We also offer climbs on some of today’s most modern routes in the Oisans Massif, the Mont Blanc and the incredible limestone of the Aravis.

Pitch after pitch, as the ground seems further and further away, the thrill of climbing and that of the vertical world will be at your fingertips.Skiing  Whether you snowboard, telemark or ski, off-piste is the soul of all mountain riding.
Instead of the tamed conditions found on piste, off-piste is a return to the wild....

To master off-piste, you need to adapt your riding to the snow and terrain, and develop a feeling for the snow and the mountain.
Rapid progression can be achieved with small tips that we offer.
Together we can discover the glaciers of the Vallée Blanche, the Grands Montets, La Grave and many more.

All our locations offer unforgettable skiing with our main goal : to find the best conditions to ride each day.
