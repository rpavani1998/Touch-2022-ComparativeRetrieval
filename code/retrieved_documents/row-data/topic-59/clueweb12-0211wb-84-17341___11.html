Ski & Snowboard Lessons


From the novice who wants to tackle gentle beginner trails to the seasoned expert determined to conquer a double black diamond run, all of our resorts offer world-class lessons for skiers and riders of every skill level.



Vail Snowsports School delivers instruction that offers inspiration, adventure and self discovery. We have a long-standing reputation for going beyond a typical lesson and providing transformative experiences that create memories and build life-long skills.

You and up to five of your friends and family members can have a personalized private lesson, tailored to your individual skill levels and styles. Each of our world-renowned pros is like a personal concierge, dedicated to revealing hidden treasures and remote runs, so you get exactly what you want out of Vail Mountain.

Learn-to-Ski...Easy as 1-2-3 and Guaranteed

These three-day ski clinics cover the fundamentals of skiing. The first day covers walking, sliding, turning, slowing down and stopping in a "wedge" (don’t worry; you’ll discover what that means in class!) On the second day, you'll learn to control your speed by linking your turns. On the third and final day, you'll learn to turn both skis in the same direction for tighter turns and control your speed while skiing the trails. We guarantee it or your next lesson is free.
