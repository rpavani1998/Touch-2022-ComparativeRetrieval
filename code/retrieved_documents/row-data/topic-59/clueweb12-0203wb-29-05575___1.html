Master the Mountain - Alpine Skiing or Snowboarding Camps

   

Master the Mountain - Alpine Skiing or Snowboarding Camps


North Creek



Find the skills needed to get you rippin' through the cruisers, steeps, glades, and parks.  All ofour camps include lift tickets, video analysis, feedback on your personal skiing/riding, indoor equipment discussions, breakfast & lunch, and lots of time on the snow with our certified instructors.

Reservations preferred.  Sign up with 2 friends and get your camp 1/2 price!

Adult Camps                             Rate        Passholder Rate
2-Day Camp                              $216              $166
Saturday-Only Camp              $130              $100

A sampling of your days:

Saturday, Day 1

8:30  Welcome!  Sign in at the Information Desk in the Base Lodge.

8:30-9:15  Over breakfast of your choice, meet your ski or snowboard group and determine your goals for the camp.  This could include overcoming fear of steep terrain, wanting to ski or ride faster, learning to ski or ride varied conditions, etc.

9:15-12:00  On the snow!  Warm up with a few runs and get a few pointers for working on your balance – the key to great skiing and riding!  We’ll grab some video* as coaches give you some tasks that will challenge you to ski or ride out of your comfort zone, while keeping things fun and safe.  Let’s push it a little – no more complacency – you’re ready to ramp it up!

12:00  Break for lunch with the group – hot soup and sandwich is a great midday treat!  Or try one of our Adirondack Salads!

12:45–3:30  Back out on the hill.  How about a few runs on Topridge or up on the summit?  Work on your skills and tactics for this terrain.  This is a great camp for making more difficult terrain easier and easier terrain more challenging.

3:30  Celebrate your day with a wine & cheese party!

*Video is usually taken on Saturday, but subject to change due to weather and visibility.  A review of video is conducted during the weekend to see how well you are skiing!

Sunday, Day 2

8:30  Check in with your group, grab some breakfast, and boot up to head out on the hill!

9:15 – 12:00  Jump in the gondola for some early morning turns!  Wonder what the hype is about getting in the trees?  Well, Gore’s got glades – lots of them.  Learn tactics for turning through the trees.  Or, if your group wishes, stay on the groomed terrain – this is your camp and the focus is on you!

12:00  Lunch break!

12:45–3:30  Back out on the hill for more fun and more trails to explore.  Maybe some synchronized carving to end the day – fun for all, skiing in someone else’s turn pattern and speed; adjusting your pace to keep the rhythm and flow.

3:30  Head to the Tannery Pub, have a drink, and celebrate your day!
