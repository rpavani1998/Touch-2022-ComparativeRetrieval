Samples from the New Nikon 85mm f1.8 on the Nikon D5100

by Chris Gampat on March 20, 2012



The new Nikon 85mm f1.8 is currently in my hands and undergoing quite a thorough review right now. The other day, I played around with it on my D5100. Here are a couple of quick image samples. The full review and other tests are coming, so stay tuned!

Gear Used

Also used was the OCF Gear TTL cord and the Yongnuo YN-560 II.

All images in this post were subject to minor sharpening and clarity boosts of a couple of levels to simulate real-world use.
