    'nuff said

    But seriously, I'll take a Windows PC over a Mac, yes, Macs might have less viruses, be easier to use, rarely crash, and be better for multimedia stuff. BUT Macs are 'spensive stuff, and I like the fact that with a PC you can pretty much put it together piece by piece to your own liking. With a Mac you take what Apple offers you and that's it.

    Additionally, if a part on your PC breaks outside of warranty you can browse many shops and manufacturers and pick the cheapest part with the best quality. When something on your Mac breaks after your warranty expires.... heh, I know of someone who simply went ahead and bought a PC after she was faced with what the repair bill would be like.
    Another problem is that when something is wrong you have no choice but to return it to the Apple shop you bought it at and then wait until they get it back from the workshop.

    So in short, while Macs may be better in certain aspects, I despise the sheer iron grip they have on their market.

    That and for gamers PCs are the way to go. Though someday I'm going to try one of those open source operating systems.
