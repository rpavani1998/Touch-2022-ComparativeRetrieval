 A 13 Days Kenya and Tanzania Safari

This safari takes you across Kenya's and Tanzania's topmost game reserves. It offers a guest the opportunity to discover East Africa's unique wildlife and cultural inheritance. The 13 day safari will take you to Amboseli, Aberdares, Masai Mara and Lake Nakuru National Parks in Kenya and Ngorongoro, Serengeti and Lake Manyara National Parks in Tanzania . Between Kenya and Tanzania are located the most varied and flourishing wildlife in the entire continent of Africa. This safari will present you with breathtaking sceneries and diverse ecosystems which are an additional worth stating.

 

Day 1 : Flight – Nairobi

On arrival at the Jomo Kenyatta International Airport you will be met by a representative from Edge East Africa Safaris and transferred to the Boulevard Hotel. Overnight atPanafric hotel based on Bed and breakfast basis.
 

Day 2 ; Nairobi – Masai Mara

After breakfast 0730 hrs drive through the floor of the Rift Valley via the Masai town of Narok to arrive at Kenya’s most populous park, the Masai Mara Game Reserve in time for lunch at Mara Sarova game camp .
Masai Mara voted one of the natural spectacles n the world has well over 450 species of animals. Although through July to August and September are the months when the Mara plains are filled with migrating wildebeest and zebra, there is also resident wildlife all year round. The famous Masai tribesmen live within the dispersal area of the game reserve. Dinner and overnight atMara Sopa Lodge.
 

Day 3: Masai Mara

Take morning and afternoon game drives in the Masai Mara game reserve with a visit to a local Masai village. Lunch, Dinner and overnight at Mara Sopa Lodge .
 

Day 4: Mara – Lake Nakuru National Park

At 0830 hrs after breakfast depart doing a game drive en-route to Nakuru. Arrive in Lake Nakuru National park in time for lunch at Lion Hill lodge.
At 1500 hrs take an afternoon game drive. This is a chance to visit the unique sanctuary home to a sizeable number of the endangered rhinoceros species and the Rothschild giraffe. Waterbucks as well as predators such as the lion and leopard are common view within the park. Nakuru National park also boasts a myriad of different varieties of birdlife in and around the lake. Flamingo's are also in plentiful at the lake. Later drive to Lake Naivasha Sopa for dinner and overnight.
 

Day 5: Lake Naivasha – Aberdares

After breakfast at Lake Navasha sopa lodge, leave for Treetops Lodge, one of the 4 Kenya’s Tree Hotels with a rich history of who is who in its visitors list. Arrive in time for lunch is at Outspan hotel in Nyeri. Later transfer to Treetops lodge for game viewing. Spend the afternoon watching game at the watering hole by the hotel from the comfort of your room, or bar or Restaurant. Before you reach the lodge it is advisable to have a stop to admire the 73m plunge of the Thompson Falls named after the Scottish explorer who fast saw them them in the late 1800s. Dinner and overnight at Treetops lodge.
 

Day 6: Aberdare's - Nairobi – Amboseli National Park

At 0800 hrs, you will be picked after breakfast and drive to Nairobi for lunch at the carnivore Restaurant. After lunch you then proceed south to Amboseli National Park at the foot of Mount Kilimanjaro (Africa's highest mountain) and check in at theAmboseli Sopa Lodge.. Dinner and overnight at Lodge.
 

Day 7: Amboseli National Park

At 0630 hrs take an early morning game drive then get back for breakfast at the lodge. Morning at leisure. Lunch at the Lodge. 1500 hrs Afternoon game drive. The snows of the mountain forms a majestic backdrop to one of Kenya's most popular spectacular displays of wildlife - lion, elephant, leopard, rhino, cheetah, buffalo and host of plains game, creating Kenya's most sought after photographer's paradise. After lunch proceed for an afternoon game drive in the Park. Dinner and overnight at Amboseli Sopa Lodge.

 

Day 8: Amboseli - Arusha - Lake Manyara National Park

After breakfast at Amboseli Serena lodge/Sentrim Amboseli, at 0800 Hrs drive through the Namanga border post into Tanzania and drive to Arusha town to arrive in time for lunch.
At 1400 hrs, after lunch proceed to Lake Manyara National Park and check in at at Kirurumu Tented lodge. Birdlife in the park is very broad. The home of tree-climbing lion, the park is also home to giraffes, hippo, zebra, and buffalo. Dinner and overnight at Kirurumu Tented lodge.

 

Day 9 Lake Manayara - Serengeti National Reserve

After breakfast, take a game drive in the Lake Manayara National Park and later drive to Serengeti National reserve.
Arrive at 1300 hrs in time for Lunch at the Serengeti Sopa Lodge. At 1500 hrs depart for an afternoon game drive n Serengeti. Dinner and overnight at Seronera Wildlife Lodge.

 

Day 10 : Serengeti National Reserve

At 0630 hrs take an early morning game drive. Breakfast at the lodge Morning at your leisure.
1230 hrs Lunch at the Seronera Wildlife Lodge.
At 1500 hrs take an afternoon game drive. Serengeti is Africa's largest national park and also one of its most popular. The park has an large number of animals, wildebeests being the most populous followed by the zebra. Elephants are notably scarce. Cheetahs and the rest of the big cats are regularly sighted in the plains. The few remaining endangered black rhino are restricted to the Moru Koppies area. The park is also famous for the wildebeests migration, often described as one of the most impressive spectacle in the natural world. Dinner and overnight atSeronera Wildlife Lodge.
 

Day 11: Serengeti - Ngorongoro Conservation Area

After breakfast take a game drive en-route to the Ngorongoro Conservation Area. Lunch at the Ngorongoro Sopa Lodge. Spend the afternoon at your lesuire. Dinner and overnight atBougaivillea Safari Lodge.

 

Day 12: Ngorongoro Conservation Area

After early breakfast descend into the crater floor for a day's game drive with picnic lunches. Ngorongoro Crater, is one the largest intact caldera in the world and one of the most spectacular sights in the whole of Africa. The Olduvai Gorge in the neighbourhood is the site where some off Africa's oldest hominid fossils were found. The Crater has one of the largest predator populations in Africa. Elephant, lion, zebra, wildebeest and a host of other resident animals are to be found here. Leave in late afternoon fro dinner and overnight atBougainvillea Safari Lodge.

 

Day 13: Ngorongoro - Arusha –Nairobi

After breakfast depart at 0830 hrs for a drive back to arrive in Arusha in time for lunch. After lunch continue to Nairobi on board a regular shuttle bus and drop off at Panafric hotel. Edge East Africa safaris representative will pick you up from Panafric hotel and drive to Carnivore Restaurant for dinner. This restaurant is famous for its succulent steaks as welcome or farewell dinner. Later you will be transferred to the Jomo Kenyatta International airport for your flight home.
