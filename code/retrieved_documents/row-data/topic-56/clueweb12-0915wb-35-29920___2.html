Whether its Mount kenya trekking safaris, Mount Kilimanjaro climbing safaris, wildebeest migration safaris, or a combination of Kenya and Tanzania adventure vacations, our travel style sums up thereal adventures of East africa travel.

Our offers in Kenyacultural tours or kenya adventure safaris, tanzania budget adventures or Tanzania lodge safaris and all Uganda adventure safaris are organized to cater for all budget adventure safari travellers and luxury lodge holiday lovers for the best of Kenya safaris, Tanzania adventures andUganda vacations.
