7 Days Amboseli Budget Camping Safari and Zanzibar Holidays

Day 1: Nairobi/Amboseli

Pick up from the Hotel at 7.30 am drive from Nairobi to Amboseli via Namanga Boarder. Arrive at the Park in time for Lunch, in the afternoon explore the park searching for the wild animals, later drive back for Dinner and Overnight Amboseli budget camping.

Day 2: Amboseli
Whole day spent in the park viewing game in this second most popular park in Kenya. Here you have a good opportunity to see all the 'Big Five' and a variety of other game with the snow-capped Mt. Kilimanjaro - the highest in Africa - in the background and also visiting the Maasai village to catch a glimpse of their way of life. Dinner and overnight Amboseli budget camping.

Day 3: Amboseli/Nairobi
An early morning game drive,back in the camp for breakfast, afterwards breaking the camp and returning to Nairobi arriving there in the afternoon, Transfer to the Hotel for an overnight.

 

Day 4: Nairobi-Zanzibar
Wake up fresh and feeling happy after the previous adventurous Kenya safari,You will be transferred after your morning breakfast to the airstrip for your Zanzibar flight. Dinner and overnight in Zanzibar hotel/lodge.

Day 5: Zanzibar.

Full day of relaxation on the white sandy beach. Dinner and overnight at Ocean Paradise Resort.

Day 6: Zanzibar.

Full day of relaxation on the white sandy beach. Dinner and overnight at Ocean Paradise Resort.

Day 7: Zanzibar-Nairobi.

You will be transferred after your morning breakfast for Kisauni Airport. Your fly540 flight for Nairobi departs at 1350 HRS to arrive Nairobi, Jomo Kenyatta International Airport at 1545 HRS to connect your inbound flight back home.
