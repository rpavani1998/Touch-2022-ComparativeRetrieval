Lodge Safaris in Tanzania
Discover Tanzania in style. We offer carefully planned, comfortable lodge safaris in the wilderness areas of the Ngorongoro Crater Conservation area, The Serengeti National park and other National parks with accommodation in tented camps, lodges and hotels in scenic and attractive locations.
· 11 day Tanzania & Zanzibar Explorer

Classic Mobile Safaris in Tanzania
This is a safari that will rival anything in Africa for those who enjoy the excitement of wild and remote places!
· 9 day Classic Northern Tanzania

Camping Safaris in Tanzania
We provide quality camping safaris with very limited participation and no participation for the discerning traveller to Tanzania. These scheduled safaris with accommodation in spacious dome tents are aimed at travellers wanting to get close to nature and wildlife. The guide and assistant/ cook take care of the camp duties. On some of the safaris clients have to put up and take down their own tents. The clients' limited participation and involvement is the secret of an enjoyable and successful safari. A real safari in Africa awaits every participant!
· 15 day Tanzania Bush and Beach Safari
