Day 1: Nairobi/ Samburu
0930 hrs: Drive north via Mt. Kenya past coffee plantations to Samburu Game Reserve noted for the unusual reticulated Giraffe, Grevy's Zebra, Gerunuk and Somali ostrich. Picnic lunch and game drive en-route. Dinner and overnight at the camp.

Day 2: Samburu
Another full day in Samburu Game Reserve with early morning and late afternoon game viewing. Meals and overnight at the Camp.

Day 3: Samburu- L.Nakuru
Leave after breakfast with game drive en route. Drive past the northern tip of the Aberdare Mountains to descend into the Great Rift Valley where L. Nakuru is located. Picnic lunch en-route arriving late in the afternoon. Meals and overnight at the camp.

Day 4: Nakuru/Nairobi
After early morning breakfast proceed for a game drive in L.Nakuru National park known for it’s millions of flamingoes and other water birds. Leave Nakuru after lunch for Nairobi arriving mid or late afternoon for overnight at Nairobi budget hotel.

Day 5 : Nairobi/Arusha.
Pick up from your hotels in Nairobi at 7.30 am drive to Arusha with a stopover at the border post for imigration formalities,arrive in Arusha to be met by our Tanzania staff followed by safari briefing, overnight in Arusha Hotel.

Day 6. Arusha - Manyara.
Depart Arusha in the morning for Lake Manyara budget safari, Afternoon game drive , Dinner and overnight  budget camping.


Day 7. Manyara - Ngorongoro.
After breakfast, proceed for an adventure drive, 2000ft down to the crater, enjoy your picnic lunch at the crater, ascend back to the campsite for dinner and overnight.


Day 8. Ngorongoro - Serengeti.
After breakfast depart for Serengeti plains, passing through Olduvai Gorge the site of the Leakey family discovery of fossils of human and animal ancestors dating back to almost two million years . Meals and overnight  budget camping..


Day 9. Serengeti.
Full day in Serengeti plains, enjoying this wild Tanzania adventure  game you might have missed the previous game drive. Morning and after noon final safari touches. Meals and overnight  budget camping.


Day 10. Serengeti - Arusha..
After breakfast depart for Arusha arriving by lunch time.
