Adventure Travel Tanzania

The Tanzania adventure safari destinations listed on this page are all unique in a away. We visit  unique Tanzania parks offering Tanzania adventure attractions  of a lifetime.

Lake Manyara national park forms part of Tanzania adventure safari destination because of its tree-climbing lions and crowned eagles. The lions are chased to the trees by the tse-tse flies and this is what makes them seek refuge on the trees. This is one of the most preferred Tanzania adventure safari.

The Ngorongoro Crater was a huge volcano that collapsed inward millions of years ago, leaving a 18 km wide volcanic crater (caldera).  It is the largest intact unflooded caldera on earth., making it a huge Tanzania adventure destination. It is a unique Tanzania adventure safari destination because of the presence of a variety of animals at the crater floor 600 metres below.
