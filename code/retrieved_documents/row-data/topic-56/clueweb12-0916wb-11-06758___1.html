9 Days Best Of Kenya Camping and Zanzibar Holidays


Day1: Nairobi/Nakuru
Pick up from your Hotel by 7.30 am, we drive down the Escarpment via the floor of the Great Rift Valley, to Lake Nakuru. Lunch followed by an extensive afternoon game drive. Dinner and overnight in a Nakuru budget hotel.


Day 2: Lake Nakuru/Lake Naivasha
After an early breakfast, drive to Lake Naivasha with a stop over for a picnic lunch en route, view the Great Rift Valley before getting into Naivasha for a Sundowner boat ride to Crescent Island well known for its birdlife. Dinner and overnight budget adventure camping or lake Naivasha budget hotel.

Day 3: Lake Naivasha/Maasai Mara
After an early breakfast depart Lake Naivasha and drive to Maasai Mara with a picnic lunch en route Evening game drive. Dinner and overnight Masai Mara budget camping.

Day 4: Maasai Mara
Whole day spent exploring the Mara plains to experience the Mara magic! This reserve which is an extension of the Serengeti National Park in Tanzania is Kenya's premier reserve and is famed for its astounding concentration of wildlife with the wildebeest migration mainly in July and August. On this day you have a chance to experience the hot air balloon ride over the Mara plains or go for an escorted walk within designated areas at an extra cost. Dinner and overnight Masai Mara budget camping.

Day 5: Masai Mara/Nairobi
After breakfast break camp and drive as you watch out for game. Stop over at Narok to buy souvenirs or just for a cup of tea. Picnic lunch en route before proceeding on to Nairobi. Drop off at your hotel for a rest after 5 days of Kenya budget camping safaris activities.


Day 6: Nairobi-Zanzibar
Wake up fresh and feeling happy after the previous budget adventure Kenya safari,You will be transferred after your morning breakfast to the airstrip for your Zanzibar flight. Dinner and overnight in Zanzibar hotel/lodge.


Day 7: Zanzibar.

Full day of relaxation on the white sandy beach. Dinner and overnight at Ocean Paradise Resort/similar.

Day 8: Zanzibar.

Full day of relaxation on the white sandy beach. Dinner and overnight at Ocean Paradise Resort/similar.

Day 9: Zanzibar-Nairobi.

You will be transferred after your morning breakfast for Kisauni Airport. Your fly540 flight for Nairobi departs at 1350 HRS to arrive Nairobi, Jomo Kenyatta International Airport at 1545 HRS to connect with your inbound flight back home.
