Kenya & Tanzania Safari Tour

Visiting: Kenya and Tanzania

Discover and experience the landscapes, wildlife and culture in the ultimate big safari areas of Kenya and Tanzania.  From the open plains of the Masai Mara, the waters of Lake Nakuru, the swamps of Amboseli to Mount Kilimanjaros snowy peaks, Ngorongoro the Serengeti.  This adventurous safari explores the ultimate big game areas of Kenya and Tanzania.  It therefore provides the perfect opportunity to spot the big five and compare some of Africa’s most diverse scenery.
