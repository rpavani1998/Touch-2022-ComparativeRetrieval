Ultimate Kenya & Tanzania Safari - NEW TRIP

Experience a Collection of Some of the Most Renowned Wildlife Reserves in Africa
Kenya and Tanzania; the very words evoke a bygone era of romance and adventure – seas of golden grass swaying from blue horizon to blue horizon, nights spent staring into crackling campfires beneath skies starry beyond imagination, beholding the timeless drama of life and death, between predator and prey, unfolding yet again on one of earth’s last, great, wild stages. This world still exists and is waiting to be discovered once more, by you, on our Ultimate Kenya & Tanzania Safari.
  • Experience classic African safari ambience and service at NHA Luxury Mobile Camp
  • Explore five of Africa’s most renowned game-viewing hot spots on one inclusive itinerary
  • The region’s most sought-after safari Expedition Leaders enhance the entire safari experience
Location of Adventure
Kenya & Tanzania, East Africa
Group Size Limit
Approximately 12 Travelers
A very important feature of our trips is the limited group size as nature expeditions are best experienced with smaller sized groups of travelers.
Included
Internal scheduled and light aircraft flights, accommodations, meals from dinner Day 1 to lunch final day, non-alcoholic drinks at camps and with meals, house alcoholic beverages, services of NHA Expedition Leaders, local guides, and camp and lodge staff, some gratuities, airport transfers on Day 1 and final day, permits & entrance fees, and all taxes.

Not Included
Travel to and from start and end point of trip, premium alcoholic beverages, some gratuities, passport and visa fees, optional activities, items of a personal nature (phone calls, souvenirs, etc.), airline baggage fees, airport and departure taxes, optional travel insurance, and required medical evacuation insurance.


Physical Requirements
Easy/Flexible
Our walking activities are usually short and optional. We do, however, recommend that travelers who participate in these programs be of an adventurous spirit, as they will often enjoy the very closest of animal encounters and safari vehicles can sometimes offer a bumpy ride.
Important Information
This comprehensive itinerary is designed to show travelers the very best wildlife destinations these two countries have to offer. We will have two different Expedition Leaders on this trip, one for Kenya, and another Tanzania, to allow each guide to impart expert knowledge about his respective country to our guests.
Getting There & Getting Home
You must arrive in Nairobi, Kenya before 6:30pm on Day 1 and may depart Arusha, Tanzania anytime after 5pm on the final day. We can best serve you if Natural Habitat Adventures’ Travel Desk makes these reservations for you as they are intimately familiar with the special requirements of these programs and can arrange the most efficient and cost-effective travel. Please call us at 1-800-543-8917.
