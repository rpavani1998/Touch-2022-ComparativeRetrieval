Day 1. Nairobi - Amboseli.
Depart Nairobi in the morning at 8.00am and drive through Masai land to Amboseli at the foot of the snow-capped Mt. Kilimanjaro, Africa’s highest peak. Proceed to the campsite for lunch and this is where great safari adventures start.. Afternoon game drives. Dinner and overnight budget camping.

Day 2.Amboseli
Wake up for a morning and afternoon game safari in this photogenic park full of adventures,hosting a great variety of animals and birdlife. All meals and overnight budget camping.

Day 3: Amboseli/Lake Manyara
After breakfast leave the campsite for the last game drive as you leave the park for Namanga border post for immigration formalities and arrive in Arusha to drive to Lake Manyara National Park for lunch at Lake Manyara camp, followed by afternoon game drive. Dinner and overnight at Lake Manyara budget camping.

Day 4. Ngorongoro crater.
After an early breakfast, depart for Ngorongoro crater, an exciting Tanzania safari adventure destination, arriving in time for lunch. Afternoon game drive. Dinner and overnight budget camping.


Day 5. Ngorongoro/Tarangire National Park.
Take breakfast then drive to Tarangire for an afternoon game drive safari adventures, Meals and overnight budget camping.


Day 6. Tarangire/Arusha;
After breakfast enjoy a morning game adventure experience in the Tarangire National Park, Tanzania land of great safari adventures. Depart for Arusha with lunch en-route.
