Preparatory Course for IELTS / TOEFL


As the demand for pursuing higher education in foreign countries is on the rise, so is the need for proving one's proficiency in English. One has to demonstrate not only one's linguistic skills (reading writing and speaking) but also the familiarity with computers. This course prepares a student in all aspects of the IELTS / TOEFL test to enable him to pursue higher education in English-medium universities.

Course Aim:

This course aims to sharpen the students' linguistic skills, which helps them to give their IELTS / TOEFL exams with more confidence, resulting in better scores. This course enhances the ability of non-native English speakers to use and understand English as it is spoken, written, and heard in college and university settings. The IELTS / TOEFL examination formats are explained and it is ensured that the students understand the requirements of these Exams. Furthermore, the students develop their overall language ability in the four skills of listening, reading, writing and speaking. The strengths and weaknesses for further development are also identified.

This course utilizes a variety of media and texts that permit students to recognize and monitor their short comings that are hindering clear speech. We focus on improving the vocabulary of the students, by making them listen to new words, checking their meaning, keeping track of them in a vocabulary notebook and then trying to use them in conversation or writing. To attain high scores, constant guidance, advice and tips on skills and strategies that are required by students to answer the questions, are administered.

Course Structure

  • Essentials of Grammar
  • Correct Grammatical Usage
  • Listening
  • Writing
  • Speaking

Special Features

  • Well equipped classrooms
  • Audio- visual training aids
  • In house course material of international standards
  • Mock tests with feedback and recommended corrective actions
