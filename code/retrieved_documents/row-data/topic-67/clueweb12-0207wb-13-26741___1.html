English Exam Preparation Courses in London

General IELTS Courses London

This is a general English course with emphasis on IELTS preparation and can be taken for as little as one week. Classes run all day, Monday through Friday (15 lessons per week) in groups averaging 16 students. The course offers special attention to writing skills necessary for the test, and students will take practice tests and receive advice and feedback from the teacher. Specialized materials for the test are provided along with materials for the general English component.

IELTS Academic Training Exam Courses London

This 4, 8 or 12 week course is specifically designed to prepare students for the Academic IELTS, which assesses their preparedness for training at a university or college. Students will study with an IELTS workbook and take regular practice tests. They will also receive advice and feedback from their teacher. The course provides 15 lessons of weekly instruction. It will include familiarization with all four sections of the exam and the help you need to succeed. Classes run all day in groups averaging 8 students. Be prepared for a fair amount of homework.

English Exam Preparation Courses in Scarborough

Pre-foundation Course Prior to Entering University or College

This is a minimum 12 week course that will improve your language skills to a level where you can take the International English Language Testing System (IELTS) exam and submit applications to colleges and universities in the UK.

The course includes all materials, and you will receive specific IELTS preparation, including information about the exam, practice and preparation for each of the four areas of the test, as well as techniques for effectively taking the exam itself. Assistance will also be provided in choosing the best path for your academic future in Britain. The school can assist with information for students planning to go on to Higher Education in the UK and will be given advice on suitable universities/courses and help with the application procedure if required.

The course provides 25.5 hours per week of instruction. Classes are held from 9 to 1, Monday through Friday with two 15 minute coffee breaks, and from 2 to 4, Monday through Thursday. Class size will be no more than 15 students.

Required Language Level:
B1 or intermediate.

Start Dates:
Please inquire.

Exams are held most weeks of the year. If you need a specific date, please provide two or preferably three months notice.

English Exam Preparation Courses in Shrewsbury

FCE, CAE, CPE, IELTS and TOEFL Courses

These courses consist of the following weekly components:

  • 20 hours general language skills practice (maximum class size 6 students).
  • 5 hours intensive grammar skills practice (up to final 6 weeks).
  • 5 hours examination practice specific to your exam (for final 6 weeks).
  • 5 hours computer-assisted & self-access teacher-guided study.
  • Teaching materials, level test, library access, progress reports & final certificate.   
  • Social activities, internet and Wi-Fi access.
  • 10 hours additional contact time with teachers during breaks, lunch and social activities.

Courses are a minimum of six weeks in length.  Other examination courses (for example, for BEC levels 1, 2 or 3) may be arranged upon request.

Required Language Level:
FCE - A2 to B2 or pre-intermediate to upper intermediate.
CAE - B2 to C1 or upper intermediate to advanced.
CPE – C1 to C2 or upper advanced to pre-proficient.
IELTS – A2 to B2 or pre-intermediate to upper intermediate.
TOEFL - A2 to B2 or pre-intermediate to upper intermediate.

IELTS - Every Monday throughout the year.
TOEFL - Every Monday throughout the year.

Exam Dates:

IELTS – Every week (with a small number of exclusions).
