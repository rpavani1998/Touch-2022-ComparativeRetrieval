Healthier grill up

Serves 4

You will need

  • 4 sausages
  • 4 rashers of bacon
  • 4 slices of wholemeal bread
  • 4 mushrooms
  • 12 cherry tomatoes
  • 4 eggs
  • 4 tbsp milk
  • 1tbsp butter
  • 1 can baked beans

Directions

  1. Grill the sausages, occasionally turning them over so all sides cook. When the sausages are half cooked, add the bacon, mushrooms and tomatoes to the grill.
  2. Mix the eggs, milk and butter together and cook in a saucepan stirring regularly to make scrambled egg.
  3. Meanwhile cook the baked beans in a saucepan and toast the bread.
  4. Once everything is cooked to your liking, serve.
