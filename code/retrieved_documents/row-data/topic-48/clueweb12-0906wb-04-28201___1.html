12 Low Cal Pasta Recipes

Make any one of these 12 low-cal pasta recipes to discover the quick healthy goodness of easy to make pasta dishes. When it comes to cheap and quick cooking, no ingredient comes close to pasta.

 

13 Healthy Easy Baked Pasta Recipes

For a warm and comforting dinner option, reach for any one of these 13 Healthy Easy Baked Pasta Recipes. From baked ziti to baked spaghetti, these baked pasta recipes are the perfect low cost meal ideas for any occasion.

 

15 Minute Shrimp Stir Fry

In less time than it takes you to watch your favorite sitcom, you have a dinner to be proud of ready to go. This simple pasta recipe with shrimp is overflowing with flavor.

Barilla PLUS Elbows and Broccoli Bake

For a healthier update on the classic macaroni and cheese, make this recipe for Barilla PLUS Elbows and Broccoli Bake from Barilla Pasta. With whole grain noodles and broccoli, this easy pasta recipe is far healthier than most other kid friendly dishes.

 

Barilla Whole Grain Spaghetti with Fresh Vegetables

This recipe for Barilla Whole Grain Spaghetti with Fresh Vegetables from Barilla is bound to be a new addition to your list of favorite Italian dinner recipes because of how light, healthy and flavorful it is. Get your servings of vegetables and whole grains in with this dish, one of the best pasta recipes for a weeknight dinner.

 

Billy's Baked Macaroni and Cheese

Billy knows a lot about the best macaroni and cheese recipes. He has made this recipe specifically to be healthy as well as delicious. This recipe for baked macaroni and cheese will have the whole town screaming for joy. Billy know his macaroni!

Cauliflower Pasta Pisavera

For a quick and healthy weeknight meal that the whole family will love, make this Cauliflower Pasta Pisavera from Namaste Foods. This is one of the tastiest healthy pasta recipes, and it's ready in less than 30 minutes.

 

Cheesecake Factory Wheat Pasta With Mushroom Bolognese

Create the Cheesecake Factory's delicious mushroom bolognese at home with this simple Cheesecake Factory copycat recipe, using wheat pasta for a lower carb option. Also, bolognese can still be hearty and filling without the addition of meat.

Cheesy Baked Macaroni

For a baked macaroni and cheese recipe that friends and family will simply adore, give this cheese-filled recipe a try. Use whole wheat pasta and reduced fat cheese for a lighter take on Southern baked macaroni and cheese.

Cheesy Spaghetti Casserole

Your whole house will smell divine after the garlic tomato sauce of Cheesy Spaghetti Casserole simmers on your stove for an hour. This is a superior spaghetti recipe that neither your nose or taste buds will forget.
