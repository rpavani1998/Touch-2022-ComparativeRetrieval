    I had the same dilemma earlier this year but after much research i bought the D40. Its great. I recommend the D40. Only buy it if you can deal with only auto focusing with motorized lenses. The kit lens is moterized and it does a great job too.
    The feel of the D40 is much better and it doesn't feel like a toy like the 350d.

    CANON 350D= 8MP
    NIKON D50,D40=6MP

    The D40's disadvantage only auto focuses with motorized lenses.
    The D40 is also lighter and smaller than the D50 and 350D.The D40 has a flash sync of 1/500th of a second which some people don't use and is usually only available on higher-end cameras


    The 350D is good if you need to crop because you have more room to crop pictures. The 350D has an internal auto focus motor.

    The D50's advantage is that it doesn't require a motorized lens to focus. It has an internal auto focus motor.


    I would get the D40 with the 2 lens deal. Look here to see what im talking about. Both lenses that come with it auto focus with it too.

    www.ritzcamera.com/product/SLR1160.htm
