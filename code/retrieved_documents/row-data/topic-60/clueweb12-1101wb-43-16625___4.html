What to expect from Sportrends

No Write-Ups
All our sports selections will not include a write-up. This will make the service more efficient, allow more time for handicapping, and preserve the value of certain handicapping information longer. The end result is that we will have the opportunity to provide you with more plays.

Release Times & Placing your Wagers
Release times may vary day to day depending on the sport, betting market and how quickly sports-books post lines with full limits. Our experience has been to make your plays 15 – 20 minutes prior to game time to let lines settle out.

Play Delivery
All plays will be delivered with a line constraint or specified pitcher. We urge you to pay close attention to them.

Line Movement & Sports-books
It is essential to optimize yourself and be prepared to place wagers immediately upon release in order to take full advantage of this service. We also recommend experimenting with different sports-books, some move lines faster than others, so see what works best for you.
