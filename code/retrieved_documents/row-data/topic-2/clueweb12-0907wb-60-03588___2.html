Laptop VS. Desktop

 

Image via Wikipedia

If you are in the market to buy a new personal computer, you may be having a hard time deciding between buying a laptop or a desktop.

The Laptop

The laptop has many obvious benefits. The main one being that it is portable. Other benefits of the laptop include it being lightweight and using up less space in your home than a desk top.

A laptop is a good option if you are living in confined quarters. They can used on just about any surface, and can even be used on your lap (hence the name) if you not have any available workspace.

There has been a turnaround in pricing trends over the past few months. Whereas laptops were marginally higher priced than a desktop computer, some manufacturers are putting very economically priced laptops on the market. They may not have all the features that the more expensive laptops and desktop versions have but they are never the less good value for money.

The Desktop

The desktop computer is obviously less portable than a laptop. However, if you want a personal computer that will perform better, then a desktop will be your best option.

If you want a computer for tasks such as video and photo editing, and even gaming, then a desktop will be able to hold more of the requirements than a laptop. When it comes to repairs, a desktop computer is more economical to run and repair than a laptop. Desktops are also more robust than laptops. If you have small children or even heavy handed adults, then a desktop is a better option for them
