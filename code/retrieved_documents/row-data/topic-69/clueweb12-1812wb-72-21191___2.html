Boxing Legend Joe Frazier Dies at the Age of 67 [Video]

By: B-Ray  |  November 8, 2011

Photo by Al Bello,Getty Images

Boxing Legend Joe Frazier loses his battle to liver cancer and dies at the age of 67. Arguably one of the greatest boxers of all time Joe Frazier made a huge foot print in the halls of sports throughout his boxing career.But what made him the legend he was, is that did what nobody thought was possible. He showed that Muhammad Ali wasn’t invincible.

In remembrance of  “Smoking Joe” I grabbed the most brutal back to back fight there ever was. Joe Frazier Vs Ali in what is called “The Fight Of The Century”.

So sit back relax and enjoy one of the greatest fight in boxing history.
