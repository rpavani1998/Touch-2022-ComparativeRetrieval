Philosophy of the Psychology of Religion

This course provides an in-depth approach to understanding philosophical and theoretical issues within the psychology of religion and/or spirituality.  Major philosophers, theologians, and psychologists who have contributed to this discussion will be covered.  The implications of current, post-modern philosophical paradigms will be given particular attention.
