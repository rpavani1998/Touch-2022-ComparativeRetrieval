A healthier take on french fries…and just as yummy!

Posted on April 6, 2010 | Published inNutrition | Comments (0)

Your kids love french fries. You want to see your kids eating foods that are good for them. Why not try baked sweet potato fries? Sweet potatoes are loaded with Vitamins C, beta-carotene, manganese, potassium and fiber.  Preheat your oven to 400 degrees Fahrenheit. Clean a few sweet potatoes with a vegetable scrubber (leave the skin on) and slice the potatoes into french fry strips the size of your choosing. Spread out the fries on a foil-lined baking sheet and drizzle with 1 – 2 tbsp olive oil. Season with a sprinkle of kosher salt and freshly ground pepper. Bake the fries until golden brown and crisp. 3-5 potatoes may take between 35-45 minutes. You can serve these fries plain or with non-fat or low-fat plain Greek yogurt and a little lemon juice as a dip!
