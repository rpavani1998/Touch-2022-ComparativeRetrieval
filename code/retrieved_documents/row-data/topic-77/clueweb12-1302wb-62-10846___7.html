Recipe of the Month

Hazelnut-Mushroom Pilaf





Prep Time: 10 Min
Cook Time: 30 Min
Ready In: 40 Min

Long-grain Rice And Orzo Are Simmered In Chicken Broth With Hazelnuts And Mushrooms And Seasoned With Parsley And Marjoram. I created this to incorporate two of our favorites: hazelnuts and mushrooms. It combines rice, orzo pasta, and lots of goodies - very delicious! - By: STEPHNDON, Courtesy of all recipes

Servings: 6

Ingredients

• 1/4 cup butter
• 1/2 cup uncooked long-grain rice
• 1/4 cup uncooked orzo pasta
• 1/2 cup sliced fresh mushrooms
• 1/2 cup chopped onion
• 1/4 cup minced celery
• 1.5 cups chicken broth
• 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley
• 1/4 teaspoon dried marjoram
• 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
• 1/2 cup chopped toasted hazelnuts


What You Should Do:

1. Place a large skillet over medium-low heat. Melt butter, then sauté rice, orzo, mushrooms, onion, and celery. Stir constantly until rice is lightly browned.

2. Transfer the above contents into VitaClay cooker.

3. Mix in chicken broth, parsley, marjoram, pepper, and hazelnuts. Set VitaClay® cooker on "Stew" cooking. Serve when the cooker switched to WARM cycle or let the pilaf stay in WARM cycle for 10 to 15 minutes before serving.

4. Enjoy

Amount Per Serving Calories:
256 | Total Fat: 14.7g | Cholesterol: 21mg
