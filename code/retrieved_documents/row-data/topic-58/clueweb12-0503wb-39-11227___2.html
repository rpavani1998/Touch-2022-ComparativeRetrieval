    Re: Family Guy vs Simpsons

    This reminds me of the Digimon VS Pokémon debate. In that debate I supported Digimon, which in my opinion is better than Nintendo's counterpart in terms of story and character development. Here, I will go with the Simpsons.

    Some said Digimon was a rip off of the other in the old thread. For this thread I say Family Guy is a horrible rip off of The Simpsons. I cannot watch the show for long, the characters never engaged me and the jokes were of bad taste. There is too much vulgarity in comparison to the Simpsons (which is undeniably vulgar).

    The Simpsons in some episodes and even the motion picture have a moral about life. In contrast, Family Guy feeds off constant randomness and incoherence.

    Also, The Simpsons came first and revolutionized television. That show I can watch for hours in a row. My favorite episodes are the Treehouse of Horror specials. I do admit it has been going on for so long the quality of the episodes has gone down, but at least not to the level of Family Guy.

    On the other hand, Family Guy brought nothing new to TV, and was even cancelled three times (always returning to broadcast).
