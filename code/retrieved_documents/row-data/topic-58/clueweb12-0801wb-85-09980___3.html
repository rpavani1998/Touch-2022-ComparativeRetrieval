well, the thing about the simpsons is it didn't used to coast on pop culture references, at least not that I remember. The characters were very well developed and never faltered, and the stories felt relatively realistic, and were often just as sweet as they were funny. Now, it's just like they're trying to be like family guy and crank out anything that'll get a laugh. The characters don't feel like the same people, and it makes me sad, because I really liked the characters.
I'll agree they have an okay episode or two every now and again, but in my opinion it doesn't feel like the same show, and it isn't anywhere near as good as it used to be.
