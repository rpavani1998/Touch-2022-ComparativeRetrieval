    Ratings: Homer The Whopper

    Viewer ratings for last night’s Animation Domination have been released.

    Last night’s season premiere of The Simpsons was seen 8.21 million. This ranked it 3rd on the night behind Family Guy, which was seen by 10.17 million, and the series premiere of The Cleveland Show with 9.42. Fourth was American Dad with 7.12.

    Fox was 2nd among adults 18-49 on the night behind NBC with it’s Sunday Night Football.
