    Ratings: In The Name Of The Grandfather

    A total of 6.15 million viewers tuned in to watch In The Name Of The Grandfather last night, despite its availability on the net all week due to an exclusive screening in the UK/Ireland. The figures aren’t excellent, but these days we’re just happy it hasn’t set a new record low. The episode attracted511,000 viewers on Sky 1 in Ireland earlier this week, more than doubling Sky 1′s previous highest audience for any program in Ireland. A further 957,000 watched the episode in the UK. These international figures, of course, are not included in the North American ratings.

    Fox was fourth overall for the night, and third among adults 18-49. King Of The Hill had 5.49 million viewers, Family Guy had 7.45 million, and American Dad had 5.85 million.
