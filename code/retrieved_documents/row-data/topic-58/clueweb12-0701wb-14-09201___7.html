    The Simpsons Season 21 Premiere Announced

    The 21st season of The Simpsons will premiere on Sunday, September 27th, 2009 on Fox at 8.00pm.

    This will be joined by the season premieres of Family Guy and American Dad! in their usual respective 9.00pm and 9.30pm time slots, and the series premiere ofFamily Guy spin-off The Cleveland Show at 8.30pm.

    The 21st season of The Simpsons will see Comic Book Guy create a superhero called Everyman, which is adapted into a film starring Homer,
    “Treehouse of Horror XX,” the twentiethTreehouse of Horror episode, and will see the return of Jackie Mason who will voice Krusty the Clown’s father Rabbi Hyman Krustofski.
