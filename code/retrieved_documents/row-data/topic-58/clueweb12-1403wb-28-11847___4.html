    Ratings: Rednecks and Broomsticks

    All shows in Fox’s weekly Animation Domination last night were up on last week’s, and what’s more, a new episode of The Simpsons won the night for the third time this season, with 8.9 million tuning in, a 27% increase on last week’s 7.39 million.

    Family Guy was a close second with 8.48 million watching (13% increase); new episodes of The Cleveland Show and American Dad scored 7.2 (6% increase) and 6.4 million (18% increase) viewers respectively. Fox was 3rd overall on the night on both the Adults 18-49 and 18-34 ratings shares.
