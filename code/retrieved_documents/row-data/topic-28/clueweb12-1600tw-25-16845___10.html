More: Singapore, April 15, 2012


Remark Photographer
VH-OQA (cn 014) On 4th November 2010, the 1st QANTAS A380 "Nancy Bird Walton" as QF32 had an uncontained engine failure in 1 of its RR Trent 900 engines over Indonesia. It limped back to Singapore, landing safely with significant damage. After nearly 1 1/2 years, the aircraft has been repaired and it is seen on it's first test flight out of Singapore. From the early and steep climb angle, the pilots were obviously very confident! [Nikon D3]
