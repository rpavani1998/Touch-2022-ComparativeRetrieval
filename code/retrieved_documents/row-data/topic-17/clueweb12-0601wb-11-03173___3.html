    our house, we have two cats and a Cockatiel bird. They're all pets mom somehow picked up. She found the bird, and got suckered by the vet with the cats.

    I like cats myself because of their independence. They can and will entertain themselves. But they also come over when they want attention. Of course, I give them attention when they don't want it either.

    I don't know what you did Peench, but apparently you were way to aggressive with your cat, and probably why you got bit so hard. Next time your kitty gets spooked, make sure to talk to him/her in calm voice and already have your hand out, but out of reach, so you kitty knows you're trying to help. If your cat is ever superfreaked, I've ran into this here and there, get a blanket, and throw it over the cat, then pick him/her up. One, it's protects you from extremely aggressive cat behavior, and two, they calm down when they don't see anything. And I can annoy the hell out of my cats where they will whine and hiss at me, take swings, try to bite and so on. But they still know I'm the alpha male, so they're usually not quite as aggressive to me as towards others. I also do the same crap to my friends' pets and always come out fine, 8). And yes, I have been scratched and bit before from cats, but usually just surface wounds. The bad stuff is usually from holding the cat and the cat got superspooked all of sudden. You just have to treat them like a toddler when they're scared, and you should do ok.
