Handsome Victor is a fun-loving and active boy who is looking for calm, patient and consistent owners who will be able to offer him the routine, ground rules and affection he deserves. 

Victor is an excitable youngster who still needs to learn the value of patience and a calmer attitude towards life, as he can at times be a little too enthusiastic in his efforts to please.

Despite still being a young adult, Victor has had a difficult start in life, having returned to kennels for a second chance at finding an understanding and loving forever home.
