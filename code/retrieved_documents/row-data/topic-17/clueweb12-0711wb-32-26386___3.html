Description:
If you look closely at that little cat face, she has quite a winsome expression and the dog looks like a friendly sort as well. Very harmonious given the stereotype cartoon view of dogs constantly chasing after cats at every opportunity.

If you prefer silver over gold for accessorizing, I have the name plate itself available in stainless steel (silver tone). If you prefer the more traditional gold tone (brass) name plate, I also give you that choice.

By far, my customers have chosen the magnetic backing over the pin backing. The pin backs are just not as long lasting, plus they put holes in your scrubs, jacket or shirt. I have customers with name badges from the Magic Zoo that are over 4 years old and the magnetic back is still in fine shape. That's my 2 cents worth of advice on the topic!

I think this expressive pewter dog cat combination name tag would also make a wonderful gift for your favorite veterinary professional whether that is a veterinarian, veterinary technician, practice manager or assistant. (I know there are a lot more job descriptions around an animal hospital or clinic but you get the idea- everyone would likely enjoy an animal name badge.)   Lots of one animal name badges have personality, but the adorable pewter kitty tooth fairy name tag is really something!
