Rosie's rescue of the week - Lizzie


Meet Lizzie, a female black shorthair approximately 5 years old. Lizzie is spayed, up to date on shots, and housetrained. She would prefer a home without other cats and dogs, but is very affectionate and will wrap her paws around your neck and give you a hug when picked up. For more information on adopting Lizzie,click here.

Lizzie was left behind when her owners moved away, and is currently being cared for byMaumee Valley Save a Pet in Toledo, Ohio. They routinely donate spaying and neutering services to Humane Ohio, an organization that provides pet care services to people who cannot afford vet care for their pets. They like so many charitable organizations are always in need of funding to keep going, and I have entered them to win $1,000 in a giveaway sponsored by Discount Pet Medication.net. If you have a minute, could you give them a vote over on my far right sidebar or byclicking here, or if you have a pet charity near and dear to you, you can also enter them in the drawing. Thank you all for your time and attention to this good cause.
