Paris

Academy of Language in Paris
Learn to master French in Paris, France's historical and cultural center. Discover the city's famous monuments, charming old quarters, cafés, restaurants, cabarets, and theatres. In Paris, you learn not only the language, but experience the unique culture, literary and political life. The French language programs in Paris are designed to immerse participants in the French language. Participants gain valuable information about modern-day French culture and society and improve overall communicative skills. For further immersion in the language and culture, French culture and civilization lectures, cinema, and guided excursions are held at no additional cost. The programs are designed to provide participants with ample opportunity to learn and use French and be a part of a comprehensive learning experience. The overall aim of the program is to instill the linguistic, practical, and cultural aspects of the language as well as the French way of life.
Location: Paris
Email: info@wle-france.com

Accord
Accord French language school offers optimal conditions for learning the language and discovering French culture. Our main objective is to develop an individual's learning strategies, and enable students to transfer communication skills already mastered in the native language to the new language. Verbal communication is a priority: learning to listen and understand, to express oneself correctly and with good pronunciation. The final objective is to raise students' language level to an efficient standard of communication. This requires not only a perfect command of the language but also a profound understanding of the cultural context. Accord is accredited by CSN in northern European countries as well as Bildungsurlaub in several German regions, and registered as a Private Institute of High Education, is a member of SOUFFLE, Maison de la France, the French Youth Tourism Club, and ELITE.
Location: Paris
Email: info@accord-langues.com

Apple Languages - Learn French in Paris
Apple Languages has 3 French schools in Paris, together with schools throughout France, Switzerland, Canada and Gaudeloupe. Paris is the natural choice for anyone wishing to learn French. Our 3 French language schools in Paris, France, offer a French language program in the heart of Paris. As you learn French at our language school, you will have the opportunity to explore everything that Paris has to offer.
Location: Paris
Email: info@applelanguages.com

L'Atelier 9
At L'Atelier 9 our aim is to offer high quality French and Spanish language courses in the heart of Paris. Our courses combine linguistic instruction and cultural workshops in order to share knowledge of French and Spanish language and culture, and to help promote their diffusion throughout the world. Our method, based on the communicative aspect of the language, requires no textbooks, but rather provides a tailored-sheet system created by the teachers, adapted to the linguistic needs of the students. Without omitting written skills, we focus on conversation and its use in every day French. That's why our teaching material is centered on actual French situations and enriched by numerous documents, including songs, newspaper and magazine articles, videos, games, etc. All of our students are placed in different groups (maximum of 9 students) depending on their level.
Location: Paris
Email: latelier9@latelier9.com

Bridge-Linguatec
The school in Paris is located in a typical French neighborhood, within walking distance to famous sites like the Champs Elysées, the Arc de Triomphe and the Trocadero. The charming cafes and shops of the Place Victor Hugo are also nearby, and the school's central location makes it easy to get anywhere else in the city. The school consists of 18 classrooms located in four different buildings within walking distance of each other. The courses we offer include private instruction, group instruction, a combination of private and group instruction, and our ever-popular immersion course.
Location: Paris
Email: admissions@bridgelinguatec.com

Cactus Language France: Paris
At Cactus we are only too aware of the importance to learn French in this day and age, be it for business, education or the sheer pleasure of being able to communicate with different people and cultures. Whether you just want to be able to order "un beaujolais nouveau" or whether you really want to get to grips with the language, Cactus has a range of French courses in France to suit all your needs and budgets. We work with two schools in Paris to suit the different tastes and demands of students wanting to study here. The Executive School is located in a charming mansion house close to Place de Clichy. With its mini-group courses, personal attention and excellent study facilities, this school is aimed at business students and those who want results, and fast. The Grands Boulevards school caters to a wider range of budgets and offers an extensive range of courses in a smart and typically Parisian building. Classes are larger here, but verbal communication is still the main priority.
Location: Paris
Email: enquiry@cactuslanguage.com

CFILC Language School
A few minutes from Parc de la Villette, CFILC welcomes you to its friendly, ideal environment created to encourage you to learn French. Practical and communicative, our method focuses on contact and dialogue. It makes use of authentic written documents and audio-visual reference material, as well as daily-life contents to encourage conversation and assure constant progress. Our professors guide each learner throughout the course with maximum language use. Our efficient, dynamic method leads you to express yourself through conversation themes, play-roles, debates, daily situational contents, and amusing grammar drills, and achieve comprehension with the aid of audio material and visual recordings on French culture. Your writing will also improve through the use of exercises adapted to each level, our perfection course (advanced levels), literary articles, texts and press articles.
Location: Paris
Email: cfilc@cfilc.fr

Cours de Civilisation Française de la Sorbonne
With the intention of promoting French culture, the Cours de Civilisation Française de la Sorbonne was created in 1919 just after the First World War. Initially, it was primarily attended by Americans but it has developed considerably since then. The Cours de Civilisation Française de la Sorbonne's current Director is Mr. Pierre Brunel, Professor of Comparative Literature at Paris-Sorbonne University (Paris IV), member of the Institut Universitaire de France; its Deputy-Director is Mr. Jean-Louis de Boissieu, Lecturer at the same university. The headquarters are situated in the Sorbonne university, and about 9,000 students attend the institution each year, from all over the world. Various diplomas are awarded for successful completion of studies at the Cours de Civilisation Française de la Sorbonne.
Location: Paris
Email: contact@ccfs-sorbonne.fr

Through the many CEA program benefits we offer in Paris you can choose from academic programs offered at multiple institutions to find the one which best fits your needs. Take French language courses available at all levels, whether you are just starting your language study or looking to increase your fluency. Continue working toward your major or graduation requirements by taking a wide variety of courses taught in English. Take advantage of the services available at the CEA Paris Office including computer access and cable television, a lending library, resource binders for extracurricular activities, and student mailboxes. Experience life as a true Parisian by living in an apartment, residence hall, or homestay.
Location: Paris
Email: colleen.miller@gowithcea.com

France Langue is celebrating its 29th anniversery this year and has taught French to thousands of students from over 80 countries since its foundation in 1976. France Langue is situated near the Champs Elysees and the Eiffel tower and has expandable classroom space around the Place Victor Hugo. Our method features an accent on speaking and listening skills all while improving the students writing skills, in a relaxed and lively atmosphere where students feel free to actively participate, express their views, in role play and simulations. Oral and written exercises are used daily to learn or review grammar, and pronunciation is corrected according to each ones needs. With specialized programs: Business French, Hotel and Tourism Industry French, French Art History, French and French cuisine, Teacher Training, 50+, A la carte group programs and Internships in French companies.
Location: Paris, Nice
Email: paris@france-langue.fr

ESL - Sejours Linguistiques
ESL - Sejours linguistiques is specialised since over a decade in the organisation of French language courses in France. Our partner schools for students and adultes teach French tuition from beginner to advanced levels, year-round in France's most liked cities. A wide range of French lessons is available, depending on each language school : general French courses (standard, intensive, super-intensive), international language certificate preparation course (DELF/DALF, TCF, TEF), Business French or French culture classes. The accomodation is generally provided in hostfamilies. However, in certain cities, such as Paris, the language school Accord offers the accomodation in student residences.
Location: Paris, Amboise, La Rochelle, Montpellier, Lyon, Nice, Montreux
Email: : it@esl.ch

Institut Parisien de Langue et de Civilisation Françaises
The Institut Parisien concentrates all its efforts and means on creating the ideal atmosphere for learning and practicing French ensuring an excellent progression. Our teaching methods are structured, intensive and adaptive, with teaching materials constantly kept up-to date. In order to encourage learning French and becoming part of the French way of life we also put emphasis on the need for students to know Paris, French culture and the French themselves as a real complement to their training. Our own in-house on-line magazine Ipm@g keeps you informed of events happening in Paris and contains practical addresses while keeping you up-to-date with the latest on the French language.

Institut Rougemont Superieur
The Rougemont Superior Institute, created in 1986 is a private institute accredited by the rectorat of Paris and the Labour ministry. The school provides an active pedagogy-based teaching method, using tools designed by our team to give students the right training required to enhance the skills they need for each level. The courses of French language and civilization are for students willing to understand the French culture as a group of values (which you can see in various ways of thinking). Located in downtown Paris, the Cours Rougemont is right in the middle of historical, cultural, artistic and economic areas of the French capital, such as museums, hostels, and restaurants.
Location: Paris
Email: inst.roug.sup@wanadoo.fr

Langue Onze
Langue Onze is a small school teaching French language to foreigners, created in 1981 in the 11th arrondissement in Paris. Working as a non-profit organisation, the school is run by a team of teachers and some active members who are united by their interest in foreign cultures, languages and pedagogy. Our teaching is based on the everyday spoken language, using our own supply of worksheets created by the teachers themselves. The variety of topics covered by this material (oral and written expression, grammar, listening and reading comprehension, pronunciation) enables us to design a program perfectly suited to the needs of any given group. All year round we welcome people of different nationalities and backgrounds who wish to learn or improve their knowledge of today's French.
Location: Paris
Email: info@langueonzeparis.com

OISE Intensive Language Schools
The teaching philosophy of OISE is based on the intensive language course. For over 30 years, OISE has developed and refined the concept of effective language training whilst remaining true to its belief in the value of individual attention in order to maximise the contact time between student and teacher. Our team of course advisors will help you choose the programme which is right for you. If you are not sure which course is suitable, OISE will construct a personalised profile to suit your needs. Classes are structured to maximise the contact time between teacher and student. OISE is committed to its policy of teaching in small groups (1:1 or 1:4 or 1:8 per class) to ensure that students benefit from individual attention even within a group environment. Whether you wish to study for professional, academic or practical purposes, OISE has a course for you.
