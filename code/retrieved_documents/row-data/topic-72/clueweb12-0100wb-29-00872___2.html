Some people like to learn new languages. Some learns a foreign language as a part of their job requirement. The need to learn new languages is strong in government departments, businesses and organizations that maintain foreign relationships. Thus, for the learner, it may lead the way for further promotions and new career opportunities in Foreign Service. international organizations, travel and tourism, teaching and in translation. Learning a foreign language will be beneficial for those who frequently travel outside. While joining for a learning program, it will be better to learn it from a native speaker. The more we use the language the more we remember it. Reading, listening and memorizing the new words will easily help to master the language.
