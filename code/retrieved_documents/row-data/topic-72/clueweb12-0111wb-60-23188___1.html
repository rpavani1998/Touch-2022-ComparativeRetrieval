Welcome to Language Door - Los Angeles





 



Hola! Bonjour! Ni hao! Konnichiwa! Welcome to Language Door!  You can learn and use Chinese, French and Spanish as well as many other foreign languages at our language school in Los Angeles. Serving students in Los Angeles, Santa Monica, Beverly Hills, Malibu, Redondo Beach, Culver City and Manhattan Beach California.

NEW! Select a class from our weekly schedule, register online and have the option to pay with Google Checkout.

We also welcome students and inquiries from the Los Angeles Chinese Learning Center, offering customized Chinese classes in Los Angeles and Orange County.

Adult language learning requires small class sizes and interaction to be effective. Our classes only have two to eight students and an average of only four students!
