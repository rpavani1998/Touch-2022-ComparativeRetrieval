Speaking of movies, watched "Rocky Balboa" while TVGal was raking in the Wedding Shower gifts on Sunday...

I was all ready to mock this thing to Kingdom Come... I mean, Stallone wroteand directed it!! The cheesiest possible sequel with a fiftysomething fighter in the ring... mockery is at hand!!

and.... no.

Shit. It was actually pretty good. I'm stunned. How is that possible?????

Really. I'm not kidding. The Cinematography, lighting, editing... damn. Mix in Bill Conti's now immortal score... and I've got chills.

Well, at least the ending will be cheesy and he'll triumph predictably and we can...

...good lord, noooooooooo. I'm stunned. Wow. Didn't see that coming...

Damn you Sylvester Stallone. Damn you....

Rating (out of 100)

Metacritic: 63
TVGuy: 67

While in Vegas last week I wanted to stop by the World Famous "Welcome To Las Vegas" sign and take a photo of myself with it...

...not for any sentimental or nostalgic reason, but because it counts as a "Virtual Geocache" - a hobby of mine - and I was approaching my 100th find...

I found out after talking to my cabbie that... well, this would be difficult as it was pretty far away from the main airport entrance... which wasn't a big deal.. I didn't give it a second thought actually... well, until my cabbie forced me to address it 17 times...

What do I mean? Let's look in to the actual conversation between myself and my cab driver a week ago.... just bear with me...

Cab Driver Guy: Where to? Airport?
TVGuy: Yes. Was wondering if...
Cab Driver Guy: That's no problem. Make tons of Airport runs every day. Between here and the Resort it's...
TVGuy: Umm.. yes. But I was wondering if you could maybe do a stop by the airport...
Cab Driver Guy: Where to? I make lots of runs to the Airport everyday and I'm very familiar with the...
TVGuy: Well... I'm assuming you know where the Las Vegas Sign is...
Cab Driver Guy: Oh yeah, I've driven tons of people there... it's a very popular spot for people to take pictures and...
TVGuy: Yes, that's what I wanted to do. How far is it from the airport? I Googled it and it seemed like it was just across the street and...
Cab Driver Guy: Well, yes, but it's on the other side. A fare to the airport is $21.00. If I took you over there it would be about double... you know, around say... $42.00...
TVGuy: Oh. So you couldn't walk it from the spot you would normally drop me off at...
Cab Driver Guy: Oh no, it's on the other side. Like ten miles. I make tons of runs to the airport each day and I can tell you firsthand that it would double your fare to about.. say... $42.00.
TVGuy: ( not caring that much in the first place). Okay, just take me to the departures gate then. Forget about it. Wasn't that big of a deal. Thanks.

Well, that of course is where a normal conversation would end... unfortunately, Cab Driver Guy had other ideas....

Cab Driver Guy: I mean, I'm happy to take you over there... it's just that it will cost about $21 to take you to the airport but it will be about double... around $42 to take you over to that sign...
TVGuy: Not a problem. I'll just do it another time. Don't worry about it.
Cab Driver Guy: It's amazing how many people want their picture taken with that sign... I've taken tons of people over there... but it costs about double to do that rather than take them to the airport proper...
TVGuy: Ummm... yes. I don't want to charge my company for a $42 cab ride. I'll do it another time. Thanks anyway. ( Opens newspaper and begins to read...)
Cab Driver Guy: Yeah, that sign is real popular. Of course it costs double to go over there... about $42 dollars rather than $21.... but I'm happy to take you over there if you want to...
TVGuy: Umm.. No. Thanks. Forget I brought it up...
Cab Driver Guy: I take a lot of people over there... that sign is real popular.. but its way over there.. look there.. do you see the Strip?
TVGuy: ( Looks up )... you mean the giant wall of casinos up ahead? Yeah, I see them...
Cab Driver Guy: Well, the Sign is over to the left side and the Airport is over there to the right... it would cost you about double to go over there... I mean, I'm happy to take you over there...
TVGuy: Err... no. The Departure Gate is fine. Thanks. I'll do it another time. Let's just drop...
Cab Driver Guy: You see, the airport is over there... and the Sign is on the left... I mean, I'm happy to take you over there.. I just want you to know that it would cost about $42 for you to go over there... which is double the normal rate of.. er.. $21.
TVGuy: ( wondering if I've dropped onto the set of "Groundhog Day 2") Umm.. No. I'll do the sign thing another time. Really. Just take me to the airport....
Cab Driver: If you want, I can take you over there... but it would cost you double... I mean, I can take you there but it's on the other side of the airport... people love that sign...

Okay, there was still 10 minutes left before I arrived at the airport... I was trapped withCaptain Repeato in this car and apparently the drive would cost me double.. you know, around $42.00. In case you hadn't gleaned that info so far...

So rather than fight the conversation, I decided to take a different tactic the rest of the way....

TVGuy: You know, I really would like to get my picture taken with that sign. How much would it cost to go over to the west side of the airport?
Cab Driver Guy: Well, it would cost about double... it's $21.00 to the gate, so to go over there....
TVGuy: So around $42???
Cab Driver Guy: Yes, that's right. I mean, I'm happy to take you over there and....
TVGuy: So what you're saying is that it costs twice as much to go to the west side than the east side, then it costs half as much to go to the east side than it does the west side...
Cab Driver Guy: ( thinking hard) Err.. well, all I know is that it costs double to...
TVGuy: So it's half as much to go one place as it is the other, but twice as much to go the former rather than the latter. In effect a 200% markup for one trip but a 50 percent reduction by half for the travel to loop back to the starting point....
Cab Driver Guy: ( brain beginning to lock up...) Well, er... that sign is popular and I'm happy to drive...
TVGuy: I'm wondering if you went to the first place after the second place if you'd be able to cut the fare in half since the initial fare would be 50 percent of what it normally would be despite the extra travel gained from preventing the mileage to double and in effect cutting the cost in half, say... $21. Which would be less than the starting price if the route was changed to go east rather than west, unless it's a Holiday, in which case fares are double unless you're in the Continental United States. Then it's half of what it would normally be, which is double the normal rate.. which is $42.
Cab Driver: ( confused to a point where his driving is actually becoming compromised ...) I don't understand sir.. do you want to go to the sign or not...
TVGuy: (evilly) I don't think so. But how much is it if I wanted to again??
Cab Driver Guy: (happy to understand a question again...) $42!! I don't mind taking you over there but it would...
TVGuy: Maybe next time. Let's just go to the terminal, which is about $21 as I understand it. Which is half as much as going to the Las Vegas Sign. Which people like. But it's farther away. And costs more. About double. Or twice the normal amount. Which is more than $21. By a factor of two. I think. I'm not good at doing math in my head. Do you have an abacus by any chance??
Cab Driver Guy: ( not sure what an abacus is) Ummmm.... no. I don't think so.
TVGuy: That's a damn shame... a damn shame.

Silence ensued happily the rest of the trip. And yes, I did ask how much was the fare when I arrived at the gate. Surprisingly, it was $21. Which is, you know, less than $42.

Normally I would just play along according to the circumstances of the visit... but this guy was particularly offensive.. 20 seconds into meeting him he insulted anyone who made less than $500,000 a year as "ghastly".. so I decided to go all out in my "role play"...

The following conversational snippets are not embellished in any way.. really...

TVGuy: ( on the way up in the elevator) I'd like to see a finished kitchen in a unit.. cooking is very important to me...
Real Estate Guy "Bob": Really? Are you trained in any particular discipline? Or do you just not enjoy eating out?
TVGuy: Have you heard of the meat product "SPAM"?
Bob: Yes, but I've never...
TVGuy: My father invented it... processed meats are very important to my family...
Bob: ( momentarily speechless) Well.. our kitchens are very state-of-the...
TVGuy: If they aren't acceptable to me, a 2nd generation SPAM heir, then I doubt that we'll be here very long...


Moments later.. in the first unit he was showing me...

TVGuy: So, this is the kitchen?



















Bob: Yes, this is the kitchen... the cabinets are...
TVGuy: I'm assuming they are Eucalyptus wood, which is standard in buildings of this ilk (I'd never used the word "Ilk" before... I was pretty proud of myself at this point...)
Bob: Umm... I'm not sure of the material, but I can call the builder personally at home and...
TVGuy: It's irrelevant... take me to the deck... where my parties will be held...

























( admittedly this building is pretty sweet.. but for $645,000 it should be...I was about to let him off the hook but this nugget popped out next..)

Bob: This building is predominately Caucasian.. which I assume would be to your liking... none of "their music" that can ruin an evening...
TVGuy: ( pausing to contemplate what was just said...) ..Yes... I despise "their music"... especially anything that mentions a "booty"...
Bob: Yes, I hate... "booty" as well..
TVGuy: ( chuckling inwardly): I have seen enough here.. take me to another of your luxury condominiums immediately...




















TVGuy: ( arriving at new condo)... well... this kitchen Clearly is equipped with Eucalyptus cabinetry (even though to me it looked exactly the same)... you do accept Cash at closing don't you?? My father doesn't like to finance anything.. unless it's a boat... and my fiance hates dealing with paperwork...

























Bob: (momentarily stunned..) Umm.. yes, Cash is acceptable... I didn't know you were engaged.. Congratulations! When's the wedding?
TVGuy: Well, one of her friends is in legal trouble right now.. so we have to finish that before we can go ahead with the wedding plans.. I assume they'll be in Monte Carlo... I've told her it can be anywhere she wants...
B ob: ( seeing huge dollar signs in his eyes ..) I hope the legal trouble isn't too serious...
TVGuy: No., the Hilton family has fantastic legal counsel... I expect Paris to be out of jail anytime now...
Bob: ( gulping) Paris Hilton? That's your fiance's friend?
TVGuy: Unfortunately.. I assume we'll have to invite the entire Hilton family to the festivities... and since they are friends with the Trumps... god, we'll have to invite them as well.. I know they are looking in Chicago for a place to lay over on flights.. I'll give them your card.. you do have a card, don't you??
Bob: Yes.. er... of course.. it may be down in my office... perhaps on our way out...
TVGuy: Whatever... I've seen what I need to... I'm prepared to sign some documents... I assume they are ready down in your financial office...
Bob: Er.. well, no... we'll have to...
TVGuy: I see.. I'll be back here with my fiance on Saturday... will you have your lawyers be here then to smooth out any difficulties? I don't enjoy my Aston-Martin to be parked just anywhere... (It's important to note at this point that I'm dressed in a 12 dollar t-shirt and Eddie Bauer shorts with Nike Tennis shoes...)
Bob: Of course.,... why don't you give me a number that I can reach you at to set everything up to your satisfaction and...
TVGuy: Don't insult me.. I'll have my representatives call you... I hope it's not a problem if I buy 2 or more units at the same time.. I don't like to dawdle ( Clearly at this point the Academy Award is mine... even I can't believe myself...)
Bob: ( elevator opens); Let me get you my card Mr... what was your name again?
TVGuy: Varnson.. Kel Varnson... ( hoping he isn't a Seinfeld fan...) You can reach me at Trump Tower in New York starting tomorrow... I'll have my secretary call you this afternoon with details..
Bob: Let me get you my card then...
TVGuy: That won't be necessary... "Bob"... I'll contactyou.. Good Day... ( walks out of building...)

I may never do this again.. clearly I don't think I can top this... but wherever there is a "Bob" out there to be scolded verbally... TVGuy will not rest...

..until next time...
