Soda Pop

Okay so I'm warning you in advance that this entry is going to go awry quickly.  It will be like spending the day with an ADD kid.  Oh look!  Something shiny! Which totally reminds me of one of my favorite jokes (next to - how do you make a kleenex dance?  Put a little boogie in it!)  How many ADD kids does it take to change a lightbulb?  Wanna ride bikes?! Yeah! Okay so anyway - I've decided Soda is Satan. It is evil and must be the reason I am fat (I'm sure ice cream has nothing to do with it - so ice cream is safe for now!)  Today is "no soda day 3."  And I'm totally ROCKIN' this no soda thing.  Seriously, it started because I need to drink more water.  I need to drink more water because tanning makes you dehydrated (oh pool boy?!) Plus soda is BAD.  Remember? So today, I had fast food - the worst of all fast food even.  McDonald's.  Know what goes with grease?  Soda!  Guess what I had with my chicken mcnuggets?  Water!!  I rock!  yeah me! Okay - so I know that soda and coke and pop and whatever is called different things in different areas.  So like in one part of the US it's all soda.  In another everything is called Coke.  Well, here in CA I call everything "cola" Coke.  But the servers always say, "Is Pepsi okay?" (Pepsi is WAY BETTER...but that's an argument better left for another day)  So when you're somewhere that they call everything COKE what if it's Pepsi?  Or are all establishments like Pro-Coke and Pepsi isn't allowed - and OMG RC don't even think about it!  Do they still ask people in those areas if Pepsi is okay?  Why do I care about this? So, apparently I have a new sense of clarity that allows me to really analyze - well, everything.  So today, at the bank, I saw a lady with a weird face.  Her face wasn't ugly - there was just something strange about it.  So I checked out her forehead to see if it was possible that she had a five-head and that was the problem.  She did have an unusually large forehead, but it was not caused by the usual strange hairline that most five-headed people have.  In fact, the problem with her face was that although her head was totally normal - her face was kind of small - and it was lower on her head than it should have been.  She suffered from Poor Facial Placement.  Sad.
