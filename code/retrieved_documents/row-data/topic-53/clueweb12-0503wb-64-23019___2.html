    Kyocera FK Black Ceramic 7.5cm Paring Knife

    A small paring knife for fruit and vegetables. Kyocera advanced cermics give a number of benefits: ceramic blades do not rust and do not absorb either the taste or smell of the cut items. Ceramic is the only material which stays permanently sharp. A ceramic knife only weighs half as much as a steel knife of the same size, which reduces symptoms of fatigue during lengthy cutting operations.

    Kyocera Ceramics Knives are made of the technical material zirconia, with revolutionary characteristics in comparison to conventional steel knives.
