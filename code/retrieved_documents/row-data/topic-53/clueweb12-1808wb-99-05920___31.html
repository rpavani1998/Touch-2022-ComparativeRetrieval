I think younger kids could grasp the concept as well. There is no reading required.

Also, the fish tokens don't have the tendency to slide all over the scoreboards. That's great when you're moving around matching your fish up.

How to Play:

Fish Stix is a visual strategy game. To start, players place 1 fish stick in the center of a table where all players can reach it. Give 3 sticks, 1 scoreboard, and 6 fish tokens to each player. Players take turns matching fish by color and direction. For every fish matched, a player moves her fish tokens that number of spaces on her scoreboard. The first player to move all tokens to 10 wins!
