How can I contact a grower who has trees for sale?
Contact information for members of the Watauga County Christmas Tree Association can be found under the “Members” heading on our webpage. It is best to contact individual farms directly; many can be contacted by phone and email, or through farm websites.

Can I tour a tree farm?
Throughout the year tree farms are continually buzzing with activity. Some growers simply have too much farm work to complete and are not able to conduct farm tours. However in many cases, with enough advance notice, many Choose and Cut farms will host tour groups or school children. If you are interested in a tour, please go to our “Contact” page and send us an email with your estimated tour date, how many people you anticipate and any other important details.
