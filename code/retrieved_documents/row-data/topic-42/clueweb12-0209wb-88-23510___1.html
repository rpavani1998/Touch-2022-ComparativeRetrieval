ADS-TBSL TI: Ford/Mazda & Toyota/Lexus module

October 21, 2008

Available immediately within the Solo Series, the new ADS-TBSL TI offers a cost-effective transponder bypass solution for most Ford/Mazda and Toyota/Lexus vehicles. Designed to offer greater coverage, with less SKUs, the new, more powerful "TI" combines the same functionalities from previous generation ADS-TBSL FM and ADS-TBSL TL modules and more.
